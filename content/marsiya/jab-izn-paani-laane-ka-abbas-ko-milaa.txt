
JAB IZN PAANI LAANE KA ABBAS KO MILAA
BACCHOWN KO JAMA KARKE SAKINA NE YE KAHA
JAATE HAIN AB FURAAT PA DEKHO  MERE CHACHA
KUCH DE YR AUR SABR KARO TUM PA MAI FIDAA
PAANI KI MAI SABEEL YAHAAN PAR LAGAAUNGI
ASGHAR KO SAB SE PAHLE MAI PAANI PILAAUNGI

LAHRAARAHA HAI DEKHO FAZAAOUN ME WO ALAM
TEZI SE JAARAHA HAI SOO -E- NAHER DAM BADAM
CHAAYA HUWA ALAM PA HAI ABBAS K A KARAM
AB HOGAYA YAQEEN KE PAANI PIYENGE HAM
MASHKEEZA BHAR KE NAHER SE ABBAS AAENGE
PYAASOUN KO DONO HAATHOUN SE PAANI PILAAENGE

PYAASA HAI TEEN ROZ SEY MAASOOM BEYQATHAA
PAANI KA EK QATHRAA BHI USKO NAHIN MILA
KARBOBALA KI DHOOP HAI AUR GARM HAI HAWA A
SOOKHA HUVA HAI PYAAS SEY BEYSHEER KA GALA
LABB TARR KAROONGI UNKAY TOH WOH MUSKURAAENGAY
PEHLAAYE DONO HAATHOUN KO GODH MEY AAYENGAY

YE ZIKR IS TARAF THA UDHAR FOUJ -E- NAABAKAAR
BAADAL KI TARHA CHAANE LAGI PHIR SE AIKBAAR
AHL -E-JAFAA SE BHAR GAYA MAI DAAN -E- KAARZAAR
CHILLA RAHE THE SAAR -E JAFAA  JO SITAM SHIYAAR
PAANI PAHONCH SAKE Y NA IMAAM -E- HUSSAIN TAK
MASHKEEZA JAA SAKE Y NA QIYAAM -E- HUSSAIN TAK

NAAGAAH AIK WAAR KAMEE N-GAAH SE HUWA
SHAANA JARI KA HOKE JUDA JISM SE GIRA
KUCH DOOR AUR AAGE BADE Y THE KE AAH AAH
TEGH -E SITAM( DAGHA ) SE DOOSRA SHAANA BHI KAT GAYA
DAATHOUN SE MASHK THAAM KE ABBAS RAHGAYE
DARYA LAHOO KE JISM -E MUBARAK SE BE H-GAYE

AISE ME AIK TEER LAGA AAKE MASHK PAR
MASHK -E- SAKINA CHID D GAYI THARRAGAYA JIGAR
PAANI KE SAATH BE H-GAYE U MMEED K EY GOHAR
JHUK KAR ALAM NE GHODE KI BAAGOUN ME Y DI QABAR
SAQQA -E- AHLEBAITH KE SHAANE QALAM HUWE
BIN BHAAI KE JAHAAN ME SHAH -E- UMAM HUWE

YE KEHKAY( SUNKE ) HAML AAWAR HUWE FOUJOUN PA SHAAH -E-DEEN
HAMSH AKL-E- MUSTAFA NE ULATDI JO AASTEEN
THARRAA  GAYI  FURAATH LARAZNE LAGI ZAMIN
JAANEY  BACHA KE BHAAG GAYE RAN SE SAB LAEEN
PAHONCHE HUSSAI N TEZI SE BHAAI KI LAASH PAR
HASRATH BARAS RAHI THI TAN -E- PAASH PAASH PAR

BHAAI KE SAR KO SHAAH NE ZAANU PA RAKH LIYA
THE PAAY ENTHI KHADE HUWE HAMSHAKL -E- MUSTAFA
PHI R ROKAY APNEY BHAAI SEY BOLAY SHAHEY HUDA
(FARMAYA AANKHEN KHOLO THO AYE MERE BAAWAFAA )
DEKHO HUSSAIN AAYA HAI AYE JAAN -E- MURTUZA
AB AA  CHUKAA HAI WAQT -E- JUDAAI PUKAA R LO
JAANE Y SE PAHLE BHAAI KO BHAAI PUKAAR  LO

AANKHOUN KO APNI KHOL KE BOLA WO TASHNA KAAM
SHARMINDA HAI SAKINA SE ABBAS YA IMAM
LAASHA UTHAANE KAA NA KAREN AAP EHTHEMAAM
AB HASHR TAK RAHEGA HAMARA YAH EEN M UQAAM
LILLAH BAQSH DIJIYE SHAFAATH KE NAAM SE
TAQSEER AGAR HUWI HO KOI IS GHULAAM SE

SUNNA THA YE KE HAZRATH -E- ABBAS NE KAHA
YE SAB KARAM HAI AAP KA YA SHAAH -E- KARBALA
RIQQAT KA JOSH BADH GAYA GHUTNE LAGA GALA
BHAAI KE AIK LAFZ ME THA POORA MARSIYA
BHAAI KAHA TO ROOH BADAN SE NIKAL GAYI
ZAANU PA SHAH KE GARDAN -E- ABBAS DHAL GAYI

PHIR SHEH KEY SAMTH DEKH KAY ABBAS NEY KAHA
YEH SAB K ARAM HAI AAPKA YA SHAAHEY KARBALA
RIQQATH KA JOSH BADD GAYA GHUTTNEY LAGA GALAA
BHAAI KAY EK LAFZ MEY THA POORA MARSIA
BHAAI KAHA TOH ROOH BADHAN SEY NIKAL GAYEE
ZAANU PA SHEH KAY GARDHAN -E ABBAS DHAL GAYEE

BOLE HUSSAIN ROKE ALAMDAR ALWEDAA
AYE BEKASSOU N KE MOUNIS -O- GHAMQHWAAR ALWEDA
AHL -E- HARAM KE QAFELA SAALAR ALWEDA
JAAO SIDHAARO MERE WADAADAR ALWEDA
SHAANE KATAKE BHAAI KO BE AAS KAR GAYE
BE DAST HAMKO HAZRATH -E- ABBAS KARGAYE

LAAE HUSSAIN MASHK -O- ALAM QAIMA GAAH ME
QUSHYOUN KE BAAJE BAJTHE THE  SHAAMI SIPAAH ME
RONE KA SHOR THA HARAM -E- DEEN PANAAH ME
MAATAM KI KAIFIYAT THI SAKINA KI AAH ME
BOLI LIPAT KE AMMU HUWE KYA BATAIYE
BABA BATAIYE MERE BABA BATAIYE

AB QATM KAR BAYAAN MASAAEB KA AYE HASSAN
TAR AANSUOUN SE HO CHUKA MAJLIS KA PAIRAHAN
TASKEEN -E- FATEMA KA SABAB HAI TERA SUQAN
DEGI DUAAEN TUJH KO ALAMDAR KI BAHAN
KAR ARZ SHAH SE BHAAI KA PURSA QUOOL HO
ABBAS KA YE MARSIYA MOULA QUBOOL HO
