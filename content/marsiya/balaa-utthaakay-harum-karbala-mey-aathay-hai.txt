BIHAAR MEY HAIN RAQAM YEH RIVAAYATH -E JAANKAR

DOH SHAAMIYAANEY HAIN BAALA -E QABR -E SIBTEY RASOOL
WOH EK REHMATH -E HAQ EK AUR AAHEY BATOOL

ZIYARATH -E SHAHEY MAZLOOM KAR RAHEY HAIN HUS OOL
LAHADH SEY AKBAR -O ASGHAR KAY DAAGH ROUSHAN HAIN
MAZAAR EK HAI PAR DOH CHARAAGH ROUSHAN HAIN

IDHAR AAYAAN HUVI BAS ZAINAB -E KHOOJISTHA KHISAAL
UDHAR LAHADH MEY TADAPNEY LAGAA RASOOL KA LAAL
NIDA MAZAAR SEY JAABIR KO DI BA RANJ -O MALAAL
MY JEETHA HOT HA TOH KARTHA BAHEN KA ISTEQBAAL
SHARAF HAI FATEMA KA FATEMA KI JAAYI KO
MERI TARF SEY TU JAA USKI PEISHWAAYI KO

CHALAA MAYEH RUFAQAAH JAABIR KHOOJISTHA NIHAADH

PUKAARI ABID -E BEYKAS KO ZAINAB -E NAASHAAD
GALEH LAGAAO MUHIB -E NABI YEH JAABIR HAI
YEH TURBATH -E SHAHEY MAZLOOM KA MUJAAVER HAI

DUBAARAH BAAP KO MY DAFN KARNEY AAYA HOON

TANEH HUSSAIN SEY MULHAQ KIYA HUSSAIN KA SAR
HUVA ZAMEEN MEY GOYAA QIRAAN -E SHAMS -O QAMAR
LAHADH PA TAQTHA -E TOOBAA H QAREENAY SEY RAKH KAR

SUNO BAGOUSH KAY IS DUM RASOOL ROTHAY HAIN
DUBAARAH DAFN MEREY BABAJAAN HOTHEY HAIN

YEH KEHKAY RONEY LAGEY HAAYE ABID -E BEEMAAR
