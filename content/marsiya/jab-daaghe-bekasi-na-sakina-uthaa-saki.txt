
JAB DAAGHE BEKASI NA SAKINA UTHAA  SAKI
AUR DARD -E DIL NA QAUF KE MAARE SUNAA SAKI
KHAAE TAMAACHE SHIMR KE JAB TAK KE KHAA SAKI
SIN KAM THA DUKH BAHUTH THE NA BARDAASHTH LAA SAKI
ROI TOU ZALIMOUN NE JAFAA BESHUMAAR KI
AAQIR YE JABR DEKH KE MOUTH IQTIYAAR KI

GAR RAAH KI TO SHIMR PUKAARA QAMOUSH HO
AUR CHUP HUWI TO BEPIDARI NE KAHA KE RO
GAH SHIDDAT -E ATASH SE PUKAARI KE PAANI DO
GAH YAAD KARKE RAH GAYI BABA KI PYAAS KO
SOI JO AANSU PONCH KE CHASHM -E PUR AAB SE
HAE HAE HUSSAIN KAHKE PHIR UTH BAITHI QHWAAB SE

DIL ME SAMAA GAYA THA JO SHI MR-E LAEEN KA DAR
SONE ME KAANP KAANP YE KAHTHI THI RAAT BHAR
FARYAAD CHEENTHA HAI GOHAR SHIMR -E BAD GUHAR
AATA HAI IBN -E SAAD CHUPOON JAAKE MAI KIDHAR
ZAINAB PHUPI SAMBHAALO KALEJA DHADAKTA HAI
SAJJAD BHAAI DEKHO YE QOOLI GHURAKTHA HAI

JAB PYAAS LAGTHI  ROKE CHACHA KO PUKAARTHI
DUKHTE JO KAAN SHAAH -E HUDA KO PUKAARTHI
AATHA NA JAB KOI THO KHUDA KO PUKAARTHI
JEENE SE TANG HOKE QHAZAA KO PUKAARTHI
KAHTHI THI NE CHACHA NA IMAAM -E UMAM RAHE
RULWAANE KO ADDO RAHE RONE KO HAM RAHE

AB KYA LUTOONGI MAAL NAHI ZAR NAHI RAHA
AKBAR NAHI RAJA ALI ASGHAR NAHI RAHA
RAHNE KO QAID QAANA MILA GHAR NAHI RAHA
HAATHOUN ME REESMA BANDHI ZEWAR NAHI RAHA
DOULAT HAI KOUNSI JISE ZINDAAN ME KHOUNGI
RONA THA JISKO RO CHUKI AB KISKO ROUNGI

KUBRA SE CHUPKE CHUPKE KABHI KARTHI TH I BAYAAN
KAL YE BACHE THO NAAM HAI WARNA HOWN BENISHAAN
WO BOLI AB RAHENGE SALAAMAT PA HAM KAHAAN
MAA NE KAHA QHUDA NA KARE AYE SAKINA JAAN
KI ARZ DEKHLOONGI JO PICHLI KO HOEGA
BABAKI RONE WALI KO SAB KUNBA ROEGA

AB MOUT MERI SAB KO MUBARAK KARE QUDA
MARTE HI MERE QAID SE SAB KUNBA HO REHAA
JAATA RAHE BUQAAR BARADAR KA BEDAWAA
JANNAT KO HAM RAWAAN HON MADINE KO AQROBAA
GHUL HO SAKINA LEKE BALA SAB KI MARGAYI
HYDER KI POTHI MUSHKILEN AASAN KAR GAYI
