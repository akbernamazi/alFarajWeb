JALDI NIKAL CHALO YE TAHARNE KI JAA NAHI

DO CHAAR GAAM  CHAL KE YE SONCHA WO NAAMWAR
MAZLOOM KI DUAA ME HAI HAR TARHA KA ASAR
WALLAH BARGAZIDA -E HAQ HAI YE QUSH SIYAR
KAR LIJE ILTEMAASE DUAA HAATH BAANDH KAR
TEGHOUN ME USKE PAAS CHALO JO QUDAA KARE
AASAAN HON MUSHKILEN JO YE BEKAS DUAA KARE

AAYA JO KAANPTHE  HUWE WO SHAAH -E DEEN KE PAAS
KI ARZ ASSALAAM ALIAKA YA FALAK ASAAS
MOULA JAWAAB DEKE YE BOLE BADARD -O YAAS
AANA HUWA KIDHAR SE TERA AYE QUDAA SHINAAS
ARZ USNE KI GHULAAM -E SHAH -E ZULFEQAAR HOON
BEKAS HOON BENAWAA HOON GHAREEBUD DAYAAR HOON

DO SAAHEBOUN  KE SHOUQ ME CHODHA HAI MAINE GHAR
HASRAT YE HAI NASEEB KARE YAAWARI AGAR
PAHLE THO HOON NAJAF KI ZIYAARAT SE BAHRAWAR
MANZOOR PHIR WAHAAN SE MADINE KA HAI SAFAR
PAHONCHUNGA DOULATEIN HAIN AGAR SAR -E NOWSHT ME
RASTE ME MOUT AAI THO PAHONCHA BEHIST ME

FARMAAYA AAPNE KE MADINE ME KYA HAI KAAM
ARZ USNE KI WAHI THO HAI DUNYA ME EK MAQAAM
US SARZAMEEN PA HAI MERA AAQA MERA IMAAM
BARSOUN SE JINKE ISHQ ME ROTA HOON SUBH -O SHAAM
HYDER KE JAAN -O DIL HAIN SHAH -E MASHRAQAIN HAIN
SADQE MAI US JAGAH KE WAHIN THO HU SSAIN HAIN

MERA HAI AB YE HAAL KE ZAQMOUN SE CHOOR HOON
JANGAL ME MOUT AAI HAI BASTHI SE DOOR HOON
EK QAAKSAAR BANDA -E RABB -E GHAFOOR HOON
AALAM HAI ISKI ZAAT KE MAI BE QUSOOR HOON
KAHNE ME BAAT AATHI HAI YE KUCH GILAA NAHI
DIN TEESRA HAI AAJKE PAANI MI LAA NAHI

MOULA SE HAATH JOUD KE BOLA WO DIL KABAAB
LE AAUN DOUD KAR MERE SHARBE ME HAE KUCH AAB
KIJIYE ZABAAN -E QUSHK KO TAR BAHR -E BUTURAAB
BOLE HILAA KE SAR KO SHAH -E AASMAAN JANAAB
AB INTEZAAR MOUTH KA HAI KYA JIYUNGA MAI
SAB PYASE MARGAYE HAIN NA PA ANI PIYUNGA MAI

DARKAAR JO TUJHE HO WO BE BAHR KIRDAGAAR
PAIDAL AGAR HAI TOO TO YE HAAZIR HAI RAAHWAAR
NAAQA BHI LE TERA TU HAI AAQAAH WO NAAMDAAR
SAAIL KO JISNE ROTI KE OONTOUN KI DI QATAAR
BEKAS HOON GO KE AAJ PA AALI MAQAAM HOON
SHARMA NA TOO KE MAI BHI ALI KA GHULAAM HOON

ARZ USNE KI HUZOOR SE AB HAI YE ILTEJAA
KIJIYE UTHAA KE HAATH MERE HAQ ME YE DUAA
PAHONCHA DE MUJHKO QABR -E ALI PAR MERE QUDAA
MOULA NE AASMAAN KI TARAF DEKHA AUR KAHAA
JIS KO NAHI SAWAAL WO DOULAT NASEEB HO
YAARAB ISE ALI KI ZIY AARATH NASEEB HO

JISD UM NAGIN -E QAATIM -E PAIGHAMBARAAN GI RAA
JISDAM NAGIN -E KHAATIM -E PAIGHAMBARAAN GIRAA
ROUNAQ UTHI ZAMEEN SE IMAAM -E ZAMAAN GIRAA
GIRNE PA SAB  GIROH LIYE BARCHIYAAN GIRAA
HAE HAE NA IN JAFAAOUN PA BHI AASMAAN GIRAA
ZEHRA SE POOCHIYE H QALAQ NOOR -E AIN KA
TAPNA ZAMEEN KA AUR TADAPNA HUSSAIN KA

MUSHTAAQ -E SE IR-E ZIBHA BADE AFSARAAN -E KEEN
QAATIL KAYI KHADE HUWE AAKAR QAREEN QAREEN
KOI PUKAARA HAAN KOI BOLA ABHI NAHI
AB DE IR KYA HAI SAJDE ME RAKH LENE DO JABEEN
AWAAZ DI UMAR NE HAI GARDA N DHALI HUWI
QAATIL PUKAARA AANKH ABHI HAI KHULI HUWI

SAB THAM GAYE MAGAR NA THAMA SHIMR -E BAD SHIYUM
SAR PEETNE KI JAA HAI RAKHA IS JAGA QADAM
SEENA DABA THO AUR BHI TADPE SHAH -E UMAM
SHOUR -O FUGHAAN SUNA ISI HAALATH ME DAM BADAM
QAATIL SE POOCHA DEKH KIDHAR GHUL YE HOTA HAI
HAI HAI HUSSAIN KE KOI RAN ME ROTA HAI

WO BOLA KOI HOGA MAI DEKHOON BHALA KIDHAR
AB TEGH PAR NIGAAH HAI SH EH RAG H PA HAI NAZAR
HAAN JAB SAWAAR HONE LAGA THA MAI SEENE PAR
SAIDAANI AIK QAIME SE NIKLI THI NANGE SAR
IS DAM HAMAARI F OUJ ME TO EID HOTI HAI
MUJH KO YAQEEN HAI YE WAHI BIBI ROTI HAI

SHAH BOLE AUR KOI N AHI YE BAHEN BAHE N
AASHIQ BAHEN HUSSAIN KI TISHNA DAHAN BAHE N
BHOOLI HAMAARE P YAAR ME MAA  KA CHALAN BAH EN
BALE KAHAAN KAHAAN YE MERI QASTHATHAN BAH EN
QAATIL PUKAARA AAGE BHI BAAHER YE AAI THI
SHAH BOLE JAB SINAAN YEH ALI AKBAR NE KHAAI THI

AAGE HAI WO MAQAAM KE GHASH HONGE MOMINEEN
YE SAAMNE HUSSAIN HAIN WO ZAINAB -E HAZEEN
GARDAN PA TEGH DIL ME SINAAN SEENE PAR LAEEN
ZAINAB YE HAAL DEKHTHI HAI AUR BAS NAHIN
NA MEHRUMOUN  KA DHYAAN NA PARDE KA HOSH HAI
YE SAB LAHOO KA JOSH HAI ULFATH KA JOSH HAI

WO RONA BEKASI KA WO GHABRAANA YAAS KA
WO THARTHARAANA DIL KA WO UDNA HAWAAS KA
KAHNA BILAK BILAK KE YE KALMA HIRAS KA
AYE SHIMR WAASTHA ALI ASGHAR KI PYAAS KA
LILLAH TEEN ROZ K E PYAASE KO CHO -DH DE
SADQ A NABI KA UNKE NAWAASE KO CHO -DH DE

THAMJAA QUDA KO MAAN HABEEB -E QUDA KO MAAN
ZEHRA KO MAAN HAZRATH -E MUSHKIL KUSHAA KO MAAN
SAUGAND H FAQR -E FAAQA -E AAL -E ABAA KO MAAN
APNI RASOOL ZAADI KI TOO ILTEJAA KO MAAN
SAARE BUZURG MARG AYE MUJH BAD NASEEB KE
MERA KOI NAHI HAI SIWA IS GHAREEB KE

AYE SHIMR PAAS BHAI KE AAUN JO TOO KAHE
ZAQMOUN SE JALTHI RETH CHUDHAOUN JO TOO KAHE
CHADAR BADAN KE NEECHE BICHAAUN JO TOO KAHE
BEKAL HAI SAR MAI AAKE UTHAUN JO TOO KAHE
PAANI TO YAAN MILEGA N A ZEHRA KI JAAI KO
AANSU CHIDAK KE HOSH MEH LAAUNGI BHAI KO

AYE SHIMR MAIN GALE SE LAGAALOON TO ZIBHA KAR
BHAI SE MILKE QAIME MEH JAA LOO N TO ZIBHA KAR
KUCH DARD APNE DI L KA SUNAALOO N TO ZIBHA KAR
SYED KO QIBLA ROO MAIL LITAALOO N TO ZIBHA KAR
PAANI TO BH OOKE PYASE KO AYE BADQISAAL DE
HAI WAQT -E ZIBHA AANKHOUN PA KAPDA TO DAAL DE

AYE SHIMR TUJHKO QAALEQ -E AKBAR KA WAASTHA
AYE SHIMR TUJHKO ROOH -E PAYAMBAR KA WAASTHA
AYE SHIMR TUJHKO HYDER -E SAFDAR KA WAASTHA
AYE SHIMR TUJHKO NANHE SE ASGHAR KA WAASTHA
LILLAH KAR NA ZIBHA SHAH -E MASHRAQAIN KO
MAI BHEEK MAANGTHI HOON MUJHE DE HUSSAIN KO

YE KAHTE KAHTE SUSTH HUWI DAM ULAT GAYA
SAR PEET THI YE RAHGAI SAR SAH KA KAT GAYA
YUN LAASH PAR GIRI KE JIGAR SAB KA PHAT GAYA
BAAHEN GALE ME DAALKE LAASHA LIPAT GAYA
MAA KI TARHA THI AASHIQ -E SHAAH -E ZAMAN BAHAN
YE BHAI BHAI KAHTI THI LAASHA BAHAN BAHAN
