OONTOUN KI NANGI PUSHTH PA BITTLAAYA THA HAMEY
SAR TASHTH MEY HUSSAIN KA DIKHLAAYA THA HAMEY
ABID KO DURREY MAAR KAY RULWAAYA THA HAMEY

AMMA PHIRAAKEY KUFEY MEY PHIR HUMKO KU BAKOO

CHAHLUM JO KARBALA ME BAHATTAR KA HO CHUKA
PALWAND BAIKASOUNKE TAN -O- SAR KA HO CHUKA
AUR FAATEHA HUSSAIN KE LASHKAR KA HO CHUKA
QABROU N PA SHOUR AAL -E- PAYAMBAR KA HO CHUKA
MAATAM ME TEEN ROZ RAHE SHOUR -O- SHAIN SE
ROE LIPAT LIPAT KE MAZAARE -E-E HUSSAIN SE

MISLE CHARAAGH GOUR -E- GHARIBAAN PA DIL JALAAE
PHOOLOUN KE SAATH QABROUN PE LAQT -E- JIGAR CHADHAAE
PYAAROUN KI BURD -O- BAASH KE SA AMAN JO YAAD AAYE
BE SAAQTHA PUKAARI KALEJA PAKAD KE HAAE
HAE HAE WO PARDA DAAR HAMAARE KIDHAR GAYE
BE PARDA HOKE AALE NABI DAR BADAR GAYE

MAQTAL KE AAS PAAS YE BEWOUN KI THI FUGHAAN
ZAINAB JABEEN LAHAD PA DHARE KARTHI THI BAYAAN
AYE MERE KARBALAAI BARA DAR HUSSAIN JAAN
HAMSHEER TEEN DIN SE TUMHAARI HAI MEHMAAN
ALLAH MERI BAAT BHI POOCHI NA AAP NE
ZINDAAN KI WAAREDAAT BHI POOCHI NA AAP NE

RAAZINHOON HOON JORAZAA -E- IMAAM -E- JALEEL HAI
PAR QAABIL -E- MULAAHEZA BAAZU KA NEEL HAI
NE KOI DAAD RASAN NA KOI K AFEEL HAI
BHAAI BAQAIR AAP KI ZAINAB ZALEEL HAI
PUSHT -O- PANAAH UTH GAYA BE QAANAMAAN HUWI
DEKHO YE PUSHT QAABIL -E- NOKE SINAAN HUWI

MARJAAEN SAB YATIMOUN KE SAR PAR RAHOON JO MAI
BACHPAN ME UNKI QALD KI EIZAA SAHOON  TO MAI
BAHLAANE KO TUMHAARI KAHAANI  KAHOON TO MAI
MAA HOON TO MAI PHUPI HOON TO MAI BAAP HOON TO MAI
INKA BHI DAAGH AAJ MILE YAKE KAL MILE
AISE NAI NASEEB KE QIDMATH KA PHAL MILE

MAIN JAANTHI THI SHAHR BASA HOGA BHAAI KA
HOGA HHUJOOM QABR PA SAARI QUDAAI KA
CHAHLUM KAROONGI DHOOM SE MAI KARBALAAI KA
PURSAAN BH YAA NAHI KOI ZENRA KI JAAI KA
MU DHAANPNE KO AAP HI PALLA BHI LETI HOON
AUR APNE DIL KO AAP I PURSA BHI DETI HOON

CHEHLUM THO KAR CHUKI MAI DILAFGAAR YAA HUSSAIN
AB ROUZA KIS TARHA SE HO TAYYAR YAA HUSSAIN
BETA BHI AUR BAHAN BHI HAI NAADAR YAA HUSSAIN
AAQER KABHI THO AAENGE ZAWWAR YAA HUSSAIN
TAKYA HAI KAAR SAZIYE PARWARDIGAAR PAR
ISDAM THO SAAEBAAN BHI NAHI HAI MAZAAR PAR

HONE LAGA SAWAAR RASAALA BASHEER KA
DANKA BAJAA HARAM KE WAIDAA -E- AQEER KA
QAIMA UTHAA LAHED SE SHAH -E- MASHRAQAIN KA
AUR SAB TABARRUKAATH JAAB -E- AMEER KA
TURBATH KE GIRD OONT BARABAR KAHDE HUWE
RUQSATH KO JAMA QABR PA CHOWTE BADE HUWE

ZAINAB PUKAARI KOOCH KA SAAMAAN HOGAYA
PHIR SHAHR MERE BHAAI KA VEERAAN HOGAYA
AUR MAQBERAA HUSSAIN KA SUNSAAN HOGAYA
HOO  KA MAQAAM QATL KA MAIDAAN HOGAYA
AAI MUSAAFIROUN KO MERE WO ZAMEEN PASAND
DUNYA ME JIS ZAMEEN KO BASTHI NAHIN PASAND

BHAYYA MAIN BE NAQAAB HOON RAHGIROUN KO HATAAO
ROWKKAHIN QINATH AKBAR -O- ABBAS KO BULAAO
QAALI HAI GOUD BHAABHI KI ASGHAR KO LETE AAO
SARDAAR SAARE QAAFELE KE AAGE HOTE HAIN
TAYYAR KAARWAAN HUWA AUR AAP SOTE HAIN
