DARD -O ALAM KI KIS SE TALABDAA D KIJIYE

AARAM KARTHE HAIN  ALI AKBAR MERE KIDHAR
AYE ASP -EY BAAWAFAA MERE DILKO QARAAR DE
AASHIQ KO CHAL KE UNKE BARAABAR UTAAR DE

GHODAA QADAM QADAM SU -E MAQTAL HUWA RAWAAN
YAANI NA SHAH KE JISM KO HO SADMA -E TAKAAN
EK NOUJAWAAN KI LAASH PA TAHRA WO NAAGAHAAN
BOO SOONGH KAR HUSSA IN PUKAARE YAHI HAI HAAN
BAITHA JO ZULJANAAH KE AAP UTRE Y ZEEN SE
DO HAATH KAANPTHE HUWE NIKLE ZAMEEN SE
