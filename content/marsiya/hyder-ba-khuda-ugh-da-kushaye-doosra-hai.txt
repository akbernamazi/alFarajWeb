ANJAAM ME AB KHAAT -MAYE SHER -E KHUDA HAI

WARID HU WI NAGAAH SHAB -E ZARBATH -E HYDER

AYE SAHEBO IMSAAL ALI HAJJ MEY NA HOG AA
TUM EID KE DIN ROVOGE SHABBAR SE LIPAT KAR

HAR GHAR M E SADA HOVEGI KAL HAYE ALI KI

HAALATH MERE GHAM ME NA BAJA A HOGI KISI KI
KAL AAYEGI YAHA LAASH PAYAMBAR KE WASI KI
KAL SHIA BHI SAR KHOLENGE AUR AHLEHARAM BHI
ZEHRA KI SADA AAYE KAFAN PHADENGE Y HAM BHI

ZAINAB SE RIV AYATH HAI KE THI CHAR -GHADI RAAT
MUSHTA AQ-E-QAZA A GHAR SE CHALE SYED -E QUSH ZAATH
TAS BEE H BAKA FF JAMA HUWE DEWDI PE SAADAATH
WAA ’N SHEH NE AZA DEE, YAHA KI SAB NE MUNAJAATH
GULDASTA HO HYDER SE SAR AFRAAZ HAMESHA
YA RAB RAHE DUNYA ME YE AAWAZ HAMESHA

PHIR BAAD -E AZAAN KAAN ME AAWAAZ YE AAYI
AY SHIYO  ALI QATL HUWE HAQ KI DUHAAI
IS NOUHE SE AAGHA HU VI SAARI KHUDAARI
CHEHRE PE NABI ZAADIYOU NE KHAAK UDAAYI
JANNATH SE NABI PHAAD KE DAAMAN -E KAFAN AAYE
SAR PEET -THEY  MASJID ME HUSSAIN AUR HASSAN AAYE

BABA K A LAHU PEHLE TOU CHEHROU PE LAGAYA

SHABBIR NE POOCHA TOU YE HYDER NE SUNAYA
RONA A YE HAI JAB HALQ TU KATWAAYEGA PYAARE

ABBAS KO ABBAS KI MAADAR NE BULAAYA
SADQHE KI TARA GIRD -E ALI US KO PHIRAYA
AUR BOLI KE YARAB MERE WAALI KO SHIFA DE
SADQHA YE PISAR DETI HUN WARIS KO BACHALEY

THARRAKE ALI NE KAHA GU SSE SE YE US AAH

ABBAS PE TOU SADQE MAI ABBAS PE KHURBAAN
ABBAS KI TU KOUN HAI YE FATEMA KI JAAN
BIBI KAHIN ZEHRA KA ATAAB AAYE NA TUJH PAR
SHABBIR KE FIDYA KO FIDA KARTHI HAI MUJH PAR
