
IBN -E- ALI JO BAAGH -E- ROSAALATH LUTAA CHUKA
BHAI KI AUR BAAP KI DOULAT LUT AA CHUKA
POONJI HARAM KI APNI BIZAA - AT LUTAA CHUKA
SAB KUCH BARAA -E- BAQSHISH -E- UMMAT LUTAA CHUKA
BAAQI KOI SHAHIDOUN KE DAFTAR ME AB NAHI
JUZ AABID -E- MAREEZ KOI GHAR ME AB NAHI

TANHA KHADE THE FOUJ ME SHAAH -E- FALAK WIQAAR
JO AAI CHAAR SIMTH SE PHIR  FOUJ -E- BAD SHEYAR
DEKHA KE HAI NIYAAM ME HYDER KI ZULFEQAAR
JURRAT HUWI HAR AIK KO BADH KAR LAGAA -E- WAAR
YAAD -E- QUDA ME SHAAH NE SAB KUCH BHULAA DIYA
JAB ZAQM KHAAYA SHUKR KIYA MUSKURAA DIYA

TUKDE HUWA JABEEN SE KAMAR TAK TAN -E- IMAAM
GHABRAGAYA FAR AS PA THO YE BOLE SHAH -E- ANAAM
AYE ZULJANAAH YAHAN SE HATAANA NA AIK GAAM
AB JUNG BHI TAMAAM HAI AUR HAM BHI HAIN TAMAAM
GHERE HUWE HAI FOUJ -E- AJAL SYEDA RAAH HAI
JAAEGA AB KAHAN KE YAHI QATIGAAH HAI

MUZTAR NA HOLAINGE TO LAGE NAWAKE SITAM
KUCH GHAM N AHI JO PADHTHI HAIN TALWAREN DAM BADAM
MAARE NA DAM JO TAN SE HO SOW BAAR SAR QALAM
RAAHATH KE DUKH SAMAJHTHE HAI  RAAH -E-QUDA ME HAM
PAIDA HUWE HAIN  DARD -O- MUSIBATH KE WAASTHE
YE SAB QUBOOL HAI HAEN UMMATH KE WAASTHE

MUJH KO NA QATL HONE KA GHAM HAI NA KUCH MALAAL
GHAR KI HAI AB NA FIKR AN BACHOWN KA HAI QIUAAL
JAANA QAREEB HAI SOO -E- DARBAAR -E- ZULJALAAL
YE FIKR HAI KE DEKHIYE HOTA HAI KYA MA AAI
MURSIL BHI THARTHARAATHE HAIN IS BAARGAAH ME
KYA NAZR LEKE JAAUN JANAAB -E- ILAAH ME

YE KAHTHE THE KE S AR PA LAGI TEGH AABDAAR
NEZA SINAAN KA HOGAYA SEENE KE AAR PAAR
GIRNE LAGE JO ZEEN SE IMAM -E- FALAK WIQAAR
BAITHAA ZAMEEN PA TAIK KE GHUTNOUN KO RAAH WAAR
GHASH ME JIGAR PAKAD KE BA DARD -O- ALAM GIRE
SAMBHLAA GAYA NA KHAAK PA SHAAH -E- UMAM GIRE

DI BADH KE PEEK NE PISAR -E- SAAD KO QABAR
GHOWDE SE GIRPADA ASADULLAH KA PISAR
BASH SHASH HOKE SHIMR SE BOLA WO QU SIYAR
JA JALD JAA NABI KE NAWAASE KO QATL KAR
GHUL HO DILAAWARI KA TERI SHEHR SHEHR ME
QANJAR WO KHAINCH LE JO BUJHAAYA HO ZEHER ME

QANJAR PAKAD K E SHIMR GHALA AASTIN CHADHAAE
BOLAA YE IBN -E- SAAD KE QOOLI BHI SAATH JAAE
DONO LAEEN JO LAASHA -E- SARWAR KE PAAS AAE
PAHLU SE FATEMA NE SADAA DI KE HAAE HAAE
QANJAR LIYE HUWE SITAM EIJAAD AAYE HAIN
CHOWNKO HUSSAIN ZIBHA KO JALLAD AAYE HAIN

ROOH -E- JANA AB-E- FATEMA ZEHRA NE DI SADAA
SADQE MAI IS LABOUN KE IS AAWAAZ PAR FIDAA
IS KA NAHI QAYAAL KE KAT -TA HAI AB GALA
YE SABR MARHABA MERE MAZLOOM MARHABA
TUKDE HAI JISM PYAAS SE HONTON PE JAAN HAI
AYE LAAL MARTE WAQT BHI UMMAT KA DHYAAN HAI

KYUN KAR KAHUN TATAWULE SHIMR -E- SITAM SHEYAAR
PYASE GALE PA SHAH KI RAKHI TEGH AABDAAR
GHAR SE IDHAR NIKAL PADI ZAINAB JIGAR FIGAAR
CHILLAAI MU NAJAF KI TARAF KARKE AIK BAAR
HALQ -E- HUSSAIN KAT -THA HAI QANJAR KI DHAAR SE
YA MURTUZA ALI NIKAL AAO MAZAAR SE
