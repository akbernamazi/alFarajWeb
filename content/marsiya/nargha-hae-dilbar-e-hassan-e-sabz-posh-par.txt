
NARGHA HAE DILBAR -E- HASSAN -E- SABZ POSH PAR
CHAAROUN TARAF HA I TEEROUN KI BOCHAA R JOSH PAR
DIL PAR SINAAN KA ZAQM HAI PAHLU PA DOSH PAR
SAHRE KI SAB KATI HUWI LAD IYAAN HAIN DOUSH PAR
TALWAR  RUN ME DULHE KA MU QOON SE DHOTI HAI
QAIME ME GHUL HAI AAJ DULHAN BEYVA  HOTI HAI

BEH BE H KE QOON TAPAKTA HAI HAR BAAR HAATH SE
ZAQMI HAI NIKLI JAATHI HAI TALWAR HAATH SE
AB KYA KARAIN NABARD HAI BEY KAAR HAATH SE
CHOOTI EN AAN ASPE WAFAADAR HAATH SE
QOON BAHGAYA JAHAAN SE MU MOD DHNE LAGE
GIR KAR ZAMEEN -E- GARM PA DAM TOD DHNE LAGE

SAR KAATNE I DHAR SE BADHE FOUJ KE SAWAAR
DOWDHE U DHAR SE SHAAH ALA M KARKE ZULFEQAAR
THE SAATH SAATH AKBAR -O- ABBAS -E- NAAMDAAR
DAHSHAT SE TEEN SHEROUN KI BHAAGE SITAM SHEYAAR
MAJROOH NATAWAAN KA AJAB HAAL HOGAYA
GHODO U SE JISM DULHA KA PAAMAL HOGAYA

RONE LAGE IMAAM -E- UMAM DEKH KAR YE HAAL
LAASHA UTHAA KE LE CHALE AAQ IR BASAD MALAAL
EK DO QADAM BADHA THA RASOOL -E- QUDA KA LAAL
HICHKI JO AAI MARGAYA HAI  HAI WO QUSHQISAAL
GODI ME SH EH KI PHOOL S EY KUMLAA KE RAHGAYA
BAAZU LAHU BHARE HUWE THARRA KE RAHGAYA

FIZZA NE DEKHA  SHE H KO JO AATHE HUWE IDHAR
QAIME KE SAHAN SE YE PUKARI BA -CHASHME TAR
SAIDANIO ’N UROOS SE HUSHIYAAR -O- BA QABAR
DEWDHI TAK AAI MAADAR -E- NOWSHAAH NOWHAGAR
FAAQOUN SE HAI  NIDHAAL  QADAM LADKHADAATHE HAIN
MASNAD BICHAAO DULHA KO SHABBIR LAATHE HAIN

HAALATH HAI GHAM SE GHAIR SHAH -E- QUSHQISAAL KI
HAI HAI  JAWAANI QASIM -E- YOUSUF JAMAAL KI
POSHAAK QOON SE SURQ HA I IS NOW NEHAAL KI
AAE BARAATH DEKHLO SHABBAR KE LAAL KI
RONE KA SHOUR CHARQ PA JAATHA HA I SAAHIBO
DULHA CHACHA KI GOD H ME AATHA HAI SAAHIBO

DOWDHE HARAM IDHAR SE  KHULE SAR BARAHNA PAA
QAIME ME A AE LAASH LIYE SHAAH -E- DOSARAA
NAAMOOS ME QIYAAMATHE KUBRA HUWI BAPAA
WAA QAASIMA KI CHARQ PA JAANE LAGI SADAA
BAEHNEY  QAREEB AAGAYI AANSU BAHANE KO
PAH LAYE MAA NE HAATH GALE SE LAGAANE KO

MU TAK AAE SAB HARAM -E- PAAK NOWHA GAR
LAASHA LITAKE RONE LAGE S HAAH -E- BAHROBAR
ABBAS PEETNE LAGE HAATHOUN SE APNA SAR
AKBAR KA PAAS RAKH DIYA SAHRA LAHU ME TAR
TAAZEER HAAI MAADAR -E- GHAMNAAK HOGAYA
SEENE KE ZAQM DEKH KE DIL CHAAK HOGAYA

FIZZA NE BAANDHA HUJRE KA PARDA BASAD MALAAI
LAAYE  DULHAN KO THAAM KE BANO -E- QUSHQISAAL
MITTI BHARI THI MAANG ME K UBRA KA THA YE HAAL
MAI-LI SI CHA ADAR  ODHE Y HUWE KHOLE SAR KE BAAL
DIKHLA K E ZAQM SAB JASAD -E- PAASH PAASH KE
BITHLA DIYA UROOS KO PAHLU ME LAASH KE

JIS DAM DULHAN NE DULHA KE ZAQMOUN PA KI NAZAR
KAA MPA KALEJA ASHK  BAHE SHAQ HUWA JIGAR
QADMOUN K I SIMT JHUK KE YE BOLI WO NOUHAGAR
SAAHEB UTHO HAM AA YE HAIN LAASHE PA NANGE SAR
KHOLE HAIN BAAL BEKAS -O- NAALAAN KO DEKHLO
TUM BHI HA MARE HAAL -E- PARISHAAN KO DEKHLO

SAAHEB BATAAO KYA MAIN ASEER -E- MEHAN KAHOON
BEKA S KAHO ON, GHAREEB KAHOON, BEWATAN KAHOON,
MAJROO  (ZAQMI)  KAHOON MY AAP KO YA BEKAFAN KAHOON
DULHA KAHOON KE AAP KO IBN -E- HASSAN KAHOON
KYA KAHKE ROWOON KOI BATATAA NAHIN MUJHE
TAZAA DULHAN HOON BA YN BHI AATHA NAHIN MUJHE

FIZZA NE LAAKE ITNE ME RANDSAALA RAKHD IYA
DULHA KI MAA NE HAATH ME USKO UTHAA LIYA
AUR BOLI ISKO PAHNO YE QILLAT HAI BE YVA  KA
KUBRA NE SAR KO PEET KE AUR ROKE YE KAHA
AB KYUN LIBAAS  BEYV ON KA MUJH KO PANHAATHE HAIN
DULHAN BANAKE KYA KOI BE YVAA BANAATHE HAIN
