
JAB SHAAH KO SAFAR ME BAHOTH DIN GUZAAR GAYE
SOGHRA KO GHAM HUWA KE MUSAAFIR KIDHAR GAYE
KAHTHI THI GHAR BHARA HUWA VEERAAN KAR GAYE
KYA JAANE K IS TARAF MERE BEKAS PIDAR GAYE
HAI SHOUQ DIL ME SIBT -E- PAYAMBAR KI DEED KA
KYA WAJHA HAI KE QAT NAHI AAYA RASHEED KA

PARDESIYOUN KE GHAM ME HAI JEENE SE MUJH KO PAAS
BISTAR PE MU LAPETE PADI RAHTHI HOON UDAAS
BABA SAFAR SE AAENGE JAB TAK NA MERE PAAS
TAB TAK BADAN SE MAI NA UTAAROONGI YE LIBAAS
JEETHI RAHI TO VISL -E- IMAAM -E- ZAMAN HUWA
AUR MAR GAYI TO BAS YAHI KURTHA KAFAN HUWA

AATHI NAHI HAI KUCH QABR -E- SYEDUL ANAAM
BASTHI KAHAAN BASAAI KIYA KIS JAGAAH MAQAAM
KYUN NAANI JAAN AAAP TO SUNTHI THI WO KALAAM
RUQSATH KE WAQT MUJH SE JO FARMAATHE THE IMAAM
KAHTHE THE KHAT BHI AAEGA AKBAR BHI AAENGE
SOGHRA NA ROWO HAM TUMHEN JALDI BULAAENGE

DEKHO KE KHAT NA AAYA NA AKBAR WAHAAN SE AAE
KUCH JAADESAA PADA SHAH -E- DEEN PAR SAFAR ME HAAE
KIS SE KAHOON JO SI BT-E- NABI KI QABAR KO JAAE
ALLAH MERE BAAP KO JALDI WATAN ME LAAE
SUNTHI HOON DOST KAM HAIN SHAH -E MASHRAQAIN KE
DUSHMAN BAHOT HAIN FAATEMA KE NOOR -E- AIN KE

MAANGO DUA KE SIBT -E- PAYAMBAR KI QAIR HO
ABBAS -O- QAASIM -O- ALI AKBAR KI QAIR HO
ASHNOUN KI QAIR HO MERI MAADAR KI QAIR HO
GARMI KA HAI SAFAR ALI ASGHAR KI QAIR O
AMMA KO USKA DOODH BADHAANA NASEEB HO
PHIR GHAR MELEKE QAIR SE AANA NASEEB HO

YE KAHKE MU KO DHAANP KE ROI WO NOUHAGAR
KURTA TAMAAM HONE LAGA AANSUOUN SE TAR
NAANI DILAASA DENE LAGI PAAS AANKAR
QURBAAN JAAUN ROTI HO KYUN DHYAAN HAI KIDHAR
VEERAAN KARENGE SHAHER KO JANGAL BASAAENGE
AB KYA QUDA NA QAASTHA HAZRATH NA AAENGE

CHARCHA HUWA YE SHEHR -E- MADINA ME JAA BAJAA
SOGHRA SE AAKE MAADAR -E- ABBAS NE KAHAA
ZAANU PE SAR JHUKAAE HUWE R O RAHI HO KYAA
SHABBIR AAYE SHUKR KA SAJDA KARO ADAA
NAAKE PE FOUJ TAHRI HAI WAARI HUSSAIN KI
AATHI HAI KOI DAM ME SAWAARI HUSSAIN KI

NAANI NE TAB YE AAKE KAHA HOKE SHAAD SHAAD
SOGHRA TUMHEN NA THA MERE KAHNE KA ETEMAAD
MAI KAHTHI THI KE AATHE HAI  AB S HAAH -E- QUSH NEHAAD
POORI QUDA NE KI JO MERE DIL KI THI MURAAD
DEKHOONGI AAJ BAAP KO MAADAR KO BHAAI KO
LO KAPDE AB BADAL LO CHALO PAISHWAAI KO

WO BOLI MERE DIL ME HAI KUCH AUR HI QAYAAL
JAB TAK NA GHAR ME AAEGA QAIRUN -NISAA KA LAAL
KAPDE NA BADLOONGI N A MAI GOONDOONGI SAR KE BAAL
AANE DO UNKO JIN KE LIYE HAI YE MERA LAAL
SHIKWA KAROONGI AANKHOUNSE AANSU BAHAAUNGI
BABA KO YE PHATAA HUWA KURTA DIKHAAUNGI

YE KAHKE GODH ME LIYA BEEMAAR KO UTHAA
AUR RUQ KIYA SOOE LAHAD -E- PAAK -E- MUSTAFA
NAZDEEK JAB GAYE TO YE GHUL YAK BAYAK HUWA
AAYA HAI BE HUSSAIN KE BEEMAAR -E- KARBALA
MAATAM KARO HABEEB -E- QUDA KE HABEEB KA
SAR KAT GAYA BADDAN SE HUSSAIN -E- GHAREEB KA

KAH KAR YE BAATH GHAR SE CHALI DUQTAR -E- ANAAM
NAANI BHI SAATH OGAYI JALDI ASAA KO THAAM
DEKHA HAR E K TARAF KO HAI QILQAT KA ASHDHAAM
KAHNE LAGI YE MAADAR -E- ABBAS -E- NAIK NAAM
MUJH KO TO HAI YAQEEN KE AB GHAR NA AAENGE
SHABBIR PAHLE QABR -E- PAYAMBAR PE JAAENGE
