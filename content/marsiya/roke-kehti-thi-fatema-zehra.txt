ROKE KEHTI THI FATEMA ZEHRA
SUNLO RUDAAD Y A HABEEBE QUDA
GHAM SE JALTA HAI AB JIGAR MERA
KYA YAHI AJR HAI RISALATH KA
LAB PE UMMATH KA HAI GILA BABA
QOOB PURSA MUJHE MILA BABA

AAP KE BAAD MUJH PE ZULM HUWE
MUJH KO UMMATH NE HAI  DIYE SADME
WO MASAYAB UTHAYE HAI MAINE
DIN PE PADTHE TO RAAT HOJAATI
ACH -CHA HOTA JO MAI GUZAR JAATI
AAP SE PEHLE KAASH MARJAATI

BE-SABAB MERE GHAR PE AA E BALA
KYA THA MERA QUSOOR AYE BABA
LAKDIYA LEKE AAGAYE AADA
MERE DARWAZE KO JALA DAALA
QATM JORO JAFA HUWI MUJHPAR
ZULM KI INTEHA HUWI MUJHPAR

SUN SAKO TO SUNO MERI RUDAAD
DHA -EE UMMATH NE MUJH PE HAI BEDAAD
RO RAHA HAI MERA DILE NASHAAD
HAAI KIS SE KAROO MAI AB FARYAAD
AAG LE LE KE DUSHMAN AAYE HAI
ZULM MUJH PAR LAYEE’N NE DHAAYE HAI

JALTA DARWAZA JAB GIRA MUJH PAR
KHADI DEWAAR SE THI MAI LAG KAR
RAHEM KHAYA NA MUJ H PE BA A-NIYE SHAR
PAAOU MALOON NE RAKHA DAR PAR
MUJH KO DARWAAZE SE DABA DALA
AAG SE PAIRAHAN JALA DALA

ZARB MERE SHIKAMP PE AISI LAGI
JISKI TAKLEEF BATTH’N TAK PAHUCHI
PEHLU TUTA MERA JAFA YE HUWI
DARO DEWAAR SE MAI DABTI RAHI
KOI FARYAAD KO NAHI AAY A
KOI IMDAAD KO NAHI AAYA

SHIDDAT -E ZARB SE HUWI EE -ZA
DARD MERE SHIKAMB MEY HONE LAGA
MAI IDHAR TADPI WO UDHAR TADPA
MARGAYA BATTH’N MEY MERA BACH -CHA
KIS SE FARYAAD MAI KAROO BOLO
AB MAI KIS TARAH SE JEYU BOLO

MERE MOHSIN KO HA AYE  WAWAILA
BEHAYA -O NE QATL KAR DALA
BESABAB MUJH PE ZULM HAI DHAYA
AB NAHI KOI POOCHNE WALA
MU KO ASHKO SE DHOTI RAHTI HUN
APNE MOHSIN KO ROTI RAHTI HUN

MERA PURSANE HAAL KOI NAHI
JEENE KA AB SAWAL KOI NAHI
KARNE WALA MALAAL KOI NAHI
ZAQM KA AN -DH MAAL KOI NAHI
JISM RAH RAH KE SARD HOTA HAI
MERE PEHLU ME DARD HOTA HAI

SIDDAT -E DARD KA ASAR YE HUWA
JISM SE TUTA JAAN KA RISHTA
JAAN LEVA THA DARD KA HAMLA
HUWI  BEJAAN FATEMA ZEHRA
