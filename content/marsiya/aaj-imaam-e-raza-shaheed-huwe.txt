
AAJ IMAAM -E- RAZA SHAHEED HUWE
SHAAH -E- HAR DOSARAA SHAHEED HUWE
SAH KE RANJ -O- BALAA SHAHEED HUWE
KHAAKE ZEHRE DAGHAA SHAHEE D HUWE
SHAH KE SAB GHULAAM ROTE HAIN
HAM SE RUQSATH IMAAM HOTE HAIN

THA JO MAAMOON RASHEED BAANIYE SHAR
DUSHMAN -E- QAANDAAN -E- PAIGHAMBAR
THA JO AAMAADAH ZAHER DENE PAR
SHAAH KO PHIR TALAB KIYA AAQAR
SU KE SHAAH -E- ANAAM GHAR SE CHALE
HOKE RUQSATH IMA AM GHAR SE CHALE

JAB NAZAR AAI QABR -E- PAIGHAMBAR
HILGAYA SENE ME DIL -E- MUZTAR
JAAKE LIPTE LAHAD SE PHIR SARWAR
AUR KIYA YE QITAAB RORO KAR
AIK LAHZA NA CHAIN PAAYA HHOON
RUQSATHE AAQRI KO AAYA HOON

KYNKE RUQSATH JUWE IMAAM -E- RAZA
THA MADINE ME TAHAL KA BARPA
KOI QADIMOUN SE LIPTA JAATHA THA
AUR LABOUN PAR KISI KE THI YE SADAA
AYE SHAH -E- AUSIYAA QUDAA HAAFIZ
AYE IMAAM -E- RAZA QUDAA HAAFIZ

TAY MANAAZIL KO KARKE PHIR EK BAAR
AAYE DARBAAR ME SHAH -E- ABRAAR
UTHAA TAAZEEM -E- SHAH KO WO MAKKAAR
PAAS APN E BITHAA LIYA ZINHAAR
TAB KAHA SHAAH SE YE WO MAGHROOR
AAP ISWAQT KHAAIYE ANGOOR

JABR SE US LAEEN KE WAAWAELAA
KHAA KE ANGOOR KO UTHE MOULAA
POOCHA MAMOON NE CHALE KISJAA
KAHA HAZRATH NE TOO JAHAAN BHEJAA
KAH KE YE SHAAH QAAS -O- AAM CHALE
SAR PA DAALE  ABAA IAAM CHALE

GHAR ME AAKAR TADAP RAHE THE IMAAM
ZAHER NE KAR CHUKA THA APNA KAAM
QAI JO AAI LIYAA JIGAR TO THAAM
THA NA EK LAHZA NA AAP KO AARAAM
YAAS THI BEKASI THI GHURBATH THI
MERE MOULA PA KYA MUSIBATH THI

MUZTARIB THA BAHUT SHAH -E- ZEEJAAH
GHA SH JO TAARI HUWAA IMAAM PAP AAH
HICHKI JO AAI MOUT KI NAAGAAH
PHIR KAHAA LAA ILAAHA ILAAALLAH
TAN SE ROOH -E- RAZA IDHAR NIKLI
QABR SE FAATEMA UDHAR NIKLI
