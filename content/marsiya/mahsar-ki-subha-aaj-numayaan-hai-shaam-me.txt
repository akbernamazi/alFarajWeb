
MAHSAR KI SUBHA AAJ NUMAYAAN HAI SHAAM ME
KUNBAA SHAFEE -E- HAS HR KE HAI IZDEHAAM ME
SAR NANGE ROOH -E- FAATEMA HAI AHT EMAAM ME
KHAASAAN -E- ZULJALAAL HAI BALWAA -E- AAM ME
JIBREEL KI KHUZAADIYAAN TASHREEF LAA YI HAIN
MUSHKIL KUSHA KI BETIYAA’N  BANDI ME AA YI HAIN

HAIN BEECH ME KHULE HUWE AAL -E- ABAA KE SAR
NAIZOUN PA U NKE GIRD HAIN GOUJ -E- QHUDA KE SAR
BACHCHOWN KE SAR, BUZURGOUN KE SAR AQROBAA KE SAR
ROTE HAIN WAARISOUN KE SAROUN KO DIKHAAKE SAR
SAR RAKH KE ZAANUOUN PA JO WAARIS KO ROTE HAIN
ZAALIM SAROUN ME NEZOUN KI NOKAIN CHUBHOTE HAIN

NEZE PA HURMULA KE CHAMAKTH A HAI EK HILAAL
BIKHRE H UWE HAIN CHAAND SE MU PAR JHANDO LE BAAL
AULAAD WAALE KAHTHE HAIN AYE RABB -E- ZULJALAAL
IS BEZUBAAN KE KHOON KA KIS NE KIYA WABAAL
GARDISH ME HAAE HAAE YE KISKA SITAARA HA I
LEKAR BALAAEN KAHTHI THI BANO HAMAARA HAI

NAAGAH HUVA YAZE ED KA DARWAAZA AASHKAAR
HAAZIR NAQEEBO HAAJIBO DARBAANO CHOBBDAAR
TEIGHE ALAM KIYE HUVEY JALLADH -E NAABAKAAR
MUJRAYI BEYHISAAB TAMAASHAAYI BEYSHUMAAR
AIWAAN MEY BHI RAQS KA SAAMAAN TAMAAM THA
PAR INTEZAAR -E ITHRATH -E KHAIRUL ANAAM THA

WO BOLE Y SAB AIK BAAR WO AYE GUNAAHGAAR
AYE QUSOOR WAAR WO AYE GUNAAHGAAR
JALLAD OO HOSHIYAAR WO AA YE GUNAAHGAAR
WO AA YE BEDAYAAR WO AYE GUNAAHGAAR
JAB SE KE AASMAAN QHUDAA NE BANAAYE HAIN
AAL -E- RASOOL AAJ HI BANDI ME AAYE HAIN

ROKAA UMAR NEY BADDKAY ALAM KO SIPAAH KO
MUJRAAH PAR AA JAMAAH KAY KIYA BAARGAAH KO
AUR HANSKAY DEKHA AAL -E RISAALATH PANAAH KO
AAWAAZ DI NABEERA -E SHAIR -E ILAAH KO
HAAN HAAN MIHAAR ROKHNAA OONTOUN KA THAAMNAA
AB HAI YAZEED KAY DAR -E DOULATH KA SAAMNAA

PAHONCHE MURAAD KO SAFAR APNA TAMAAM HAI
UTHRO ASEERO UTHRA ADAB KA M UQAAM HAI
AAGE THO AASTHAANA -E- SULTAAN -E SHAAM HAI
DEKHO TO KYA JALAAL HAI KYA EHTHE MAAM HAI
ISKI  VILAA ME ARSH KO HAMNE HILAA DIYA
GHAR PANJETAN KA QHAAK ME BILKUL MILAA DIYA
