AYE MOMINO YASRAB ME AJAB NOWHA GARI HAI
ZEHRA KE LIYE DAGHE GHAME BE PIDARI HAI
LAB KHUSHK HAI MUH ZARD HAI AAN KHON ME TARI HAI
PAIGHAMBARE KONAIN CHIRAGHE SAHARI HAI
HOTE HAIN JUDA KHAIRE BASHAR KHAIRUNNISA SE
MAHBOOBE KHUDA CHOOT -TEY HAIN SHE R-E-KHUDA SE

JISKA KAL MA PADHTHE HAIN SAB MOMINE DEENDA AR
AB AAZIME FIRDOUS HAI WO QHA ASAY E GHAFFAR
MAKKE ME MADINE ME TABAHI KE HAIN AASAAR
AB HOTE HAIN UMMAT SE JUDA AHMED -E-MUQTAR
FAAQE KIYE GHAMGEEN RAHE DUNYA KE SAFAR ME
AAQ HAR SAFAR -E-MARG KIYA MAAH -E-SAFAR ME

EK KAAFERA NE GOSHT ME THA ZAHER KHILAYA
US ZAHER NE AAQER YE ASAR APNA DIKHA YA
MOULA KO SARE BISTARE AAZAAR GIRAYA
HISSE ME HASSAN KE WAHI ZAHER -E SITAM AAYA
KAMZOR KIYA TAPH NE RASOOLE ARABI KO
TAAQAT H NA RAHI BAITHNE UTHNE KI NABI KO

HAI AAQRI USS WAQT MERA WAAZ -O-NASIHAT
AB MIMBAR -O-MEHR AAB SE HOTA HOON MAI RUQSAT
AANA THA MI NALLAH ILALLAH HAI RAHLATH
AB HOGI MULAQAT MERI ROZ -E-QAYAMAT
TUM GHAM ME MERE NALA -O-FARYAAD KAROGE
IN-SAAL MUJHE HAJJ ME BAHOT YAAD KAROGE

DO CHEEZEN MAIN CHO -DE HUWE JAATA HOON MAGAR HAAN
WO EK TO MERI AAL HAI AUR AI K HAI QURAAN
QURAAN KI HAFIZ MERI I TRAT H HAI NIGAHBAAN
YE DONO BUZURGI WO FAZILAT H ME HAI YAKSAAN
QURAAN TO TUM SAB KI TILAWAT KAY LIYE HAI
AUR AAL -PAYAMBAR KI HIDAYAT KE LIYE HAI

GAR BAAD MERE PAHONCHEGA ZEHRA KO KUCH AAZAAR
MARQA D S E MERI PUSHT NAHI LAGNE KI ZA NHAAR
YE CHAAR BUZURG AALE  PAYAMBAR HAIN QABARDAR
ZEHRA -O- HUSSAIN -O HASSA N-O HYDER -E- KARRAR
WAJIB MERE AS -HAAB PA INSAB KI WILA HAI
YARO YAHI TABLEEGHE RISALAT KA SILA HAI

FAAREGH JO HUWA WAAZ SE WO AASHIQ -E-DAAWAR
PHIR GHAR ME GAYE AUR GIRE FARSHE AJAL PAR
US ROZ S E TASHREEF N A PHIR LE GAYE BAAHA R
BAS QABR ME JAANE KO UTHE VA SE PAYAMBAR
HAIRAN -O PARISHAN -O DIL AFSURDA THI ZEHRA
BABA ABHI JEETHA THA MAGAR MURDA THI ZEHRA

PAR AHMED -E MUQTAR THE JAB HOSH ME AATE
ZEHRA KO BASAD YAAS KALEJE SE LAGATE
GEH PYAR SE YE DONO NAWASOUN KO SUNATE
MAIN ACCHA HOON TUM KISLIYE KHANA NAHI KHATE
WO KAHTE THE KYA QHAAK TAMANNAE GHIZA HO
ALLAH KARE JALD KAHEEN TUM KO SHIFA HO

SHAH KAHTE THE SEHAT HO NA HO KHANA TO KHALO
NANA KE NAWASOU DILE MUZTAR KO SAMBHALO
CHILLATI THI ZEHRA KE NA YE ZIKR N IKALO
BABA MERE FARZANDOUN KO DULHA TO BANALO
ARMAN THA TUM KO TO NAWASOUN KI DULHAN KA
KYA BYAAH NA DEKHOGE HUSSAIN AUR HASSAN KA

EK AAH KI, RONE LAGE AUR GHASH H UWE MOULA
DARWAZE SE NAGAAH SADA YE HUYI PAIDA
DO IZN HUZOOR AY -HARAME SYEDE WALA
JAKAR KAH A ZEHRA NE KEY, BEHOSH HAI ’N BABA
PHIR AAI NIDA PHIR WAHI FARMAGAYI ZEHRA
PAR TEESRI AWAAZ ME THARRAGAYI ZEHRA

FURSATH JO HUWI GHASH SE TO HAZRATH NE BHI PUCHA
ZEHRA NE KAHA AIK ARAB AYA HAI TANHA
WO MAANGTHA HAI IZN HUZOOR AE MERE BABA
KITNA HI KIYA UZR  NAHI USKO PAZIRA
HAZRAT NE KAHA YE MALAKUL MOUT HAI ZEHRA
HANGAM MERI ZEEST KA AB FOUTH HAI ZEHRA
