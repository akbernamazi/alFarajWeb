SAR KATA ZULM SAHE HAQ KA MAGAR KAAM KIYAA
SABR NE AABID -E- BEEMAR KE ITNAAM KIYAA
BAAP AUR BETE NE ISLAAM KO ISLAAM KIYAA
WAADA TIFLI KA JO SHABBIR WAFAA KARTE HAIN
HAQ -E- FARZAND YE SARWAR K A ADAA KARTE HAIN

KOUN MASOOM HUWA TOUQ -O-SALAASIL ME ASER
KOUN HAI WO JISE PAHNAAI HAI DEHRI ZANJEER
TAAZIYAANOUN SE ADOO DETE THE KISKO TAAZEER
KISKE MAA BAHNOUN KI MAJME ME HUWI HAI TASHHEER
TAI KOI KAR NA SAKA JISKO WO YE MANZIL HAI
SABR NAAMOOS KE BAARE ME BAHOT MUSHKIL HAI

KARGAYE THE JO WASIYAATH SHAH -E- DEEN WAQT -E-AQEER
SABR SE KAAM LIYA KIJIYE BAADE SHABBIR
SHUKR KARNA JO PAHNAE TUMHEN BHAARI ZANJEER
BAD - DUAA DENA NAA UMMAT KO JO ZAINAB HO ASEER
TUM JO BIGDOGE TO QHAALEQ KA GHAZAB AAEGAA
KAAM JO MAINE BANAAYA HAI BIGAD JAAEGAA

DHYAAN BABA KI WASIYAATH PA JO KARTHE HAI  IMAAM
UNKE QAATIL SE BHI FARMAATHE HAIN NARMI SE KALAAM
TOUQ PAHNAANE KO JO LAATHA HAI WO BAD ANJAAM
QUD BADHAA DETE HAI  GARDAN KO SHAINSHAAH -E- ANAAM
YE TAHAMMUL HAI ISE S HAAN -E- WAFAA KAHTHE HAIN
KAHNE WAALE ISE TASLEEM -O- RAZAA KAHTHE HAIN

BAAP KI LAASH PA WO ZULM HUWAA KUCH NA KAHAA
CHIN GAYI SAANIYE ZEHRA KI RIDAA KUCH NA KAHAA
BAAZU NAAMOOS KA RASSI ME BANDHAA KUCH NA KAHAA
TAAZIYANOUN KI SAHI SAQT JAFAA KUCH NA KAHA A
GHAM SE MAADAR KA JIGAR SEENE ME PHAT THE DEKHA
ALI ASGHAR KA GALA QABR ME KAT THE DEKHA

SAR BARAHNA SAR -E- DARBAAR GAYE SABR KIYA
DAR BADAR KOONCHA -O- BAAZAAR GAYE SABR KIYA
QUDRATAIN RAKH KE GIRAFTAAR GAYE SABR KIYA
BE DAWA QAED ME BEEMAAR GAYE SABR  KIYA
QADR BABA KI WASIYYATH KI DIKHAAI TOONE
QABR HAATHOUN SE SAKINA KI BANAAI TOONE

YAA ALI DEKHLO POUTHE KI MUSIBATH DEKHO
GHAM -O- INDOWH KI TASWEER HAI SOORATH DEKHO
SHIDDATH -E- TIP SE GHAZAB KI HAI NAQAAHAT DEKHO
TOUQ PHIR US PA GALE ME HAI QAYAAMA T DEKHO
WAQF -E- AALAAM HAIN HAZRATH KE GHARAANE WAALE
KYA SITAM SAHTHE HAI UMMATH KE BACHAANE WAALE

HAAE JEETHE HAIN TERI QABR MITAANE WAALE
QULD ME AHMED -E- MURSAL KO RULAANE WAALE
CHASHME INSAAF SE DEKHE TO ZAMAANE WAALE
KAISE HOTE HAIN MOHAMMED KE GH ARAANE WAALE
ZAHER PAATHE HAIN GALA TAEGH SE KATWAATHE HAIN
QABR ME JAAKE BHI AARAAM NAHI PAATHE HAIN
