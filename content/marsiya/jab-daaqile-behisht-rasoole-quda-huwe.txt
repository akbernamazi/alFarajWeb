JAB DAAQILE BEHISHT RASOOLE QUDA HUWE
YAANI JAHAN SE RAAHI -E- MULKE BAQAA HUWE
MAHZOO -WO DIL MALOOL SHAH -E- LAFATHA HUWE
SIBTAIN GHAM ME NANA KE SARF -E BUKA HUWE
SADMA HAR AIK KO THA JANAB -E- RASOOL KA
PAR HAAL GHAIR SAB SE HU WA THA BATOOL KA

GAAHEY ALI SE KAHTI THI ROKAR WO DARDNAAK
WAALI NABI KO TUM NE SULAYA BA ZERE KHAAK
KYUN KAR CHUPAYA QABR ME TUMNE W O ROO -E-PAAK
HAI HAI PIDAR HALAAK HO BETI NA HO HALAAK
ITNA TO KAHTE PAAINTI KISKO SULAOGE
PUCHA TO HOTA FATIMA KO KAB BULAOGE

BHULA THA IS QALQ ME HUSSAIN -O HASSAN KA PYAR
AUR PYAR THA TO YE THA KE KAHTI THI BAAR BAAR
KANDHE PA APNE NANA KE HOTE NAHI SA WAR
AB GESU -E- NABI KO BANATE NAHI MEHAAR
BETE TO MAA SE KAHTE THE NANA KIDHAR GAYE
MAA POOCHTI THI BETOUN SE BABAA KIDHAR GAYE

BISTAR SE THARTHARA  KE UTHEY  THAM KAR ASAA
QABRE NABI PA FATEHA -E- AKH RI PADHA
HAMSAIOUN KE GHAR GA YE RUQSAT KO AUR KAHA
LO SA HEBO BAHEL KARO AB KOOCH HAI MERA
EIZAA THI TUM SABOUN KO MERE SHOUR -O SHAIN SE
KAL FATIMA NA HOYEGI TUM SONA CHAIN SE

HAM  SAAIYAAH KE HUQOOQ SE AAGAH HAI JAHAAN
LOGO MERE YATIMOUN SE R EHNA NIGAH BAAN
AB SATH I NKA AUR TUMHARA HAI HAM KAHAN
BIN MAA KI HOT I HAIN MERI DUKHYARI BETIYAN
BACHPA NN PA APNE BA CHCHO UN KE AANSU BAHATI HOON
TUM PAR HASSAN HUSSAIN KO MAIN CHO DDE JAATI HOON

ROKAR KABHI HASSAN KO GALE SE LAGA A LIYA
AAGHOSH ME HUSSAIN KO GAAHE BITHA LIYA
RUQSATH KIYA KISI KO, KISI KO BUL AA LIYA
PADHNE KE WAASTE KABHI QURAAN UTHA  LIYA
KAHTI THI GAAH BACHOWN SE MU APNA MO DD KAR
KAL SOONE GHAR ME SONA HAI BASTI KO CHO DD KAR

SUNLO WASEEYATEY MERI IS  WAQT YA IMAM
FIZZA SE MERE BAAD ADALATH SE LENA KAAM
HUSHYAR FATIMA KI AMANATH SE SUBH -O SHAAM
DOH BETIYAN,  DOH BETE HAIN BAS AUR QUDA KA NAAM
MAIN NE BADE Y DUKHOUN SE YE SAB BACHE PAALE HAIN
HAQ KE HAWALE TUM YE TUMHARE HAWALE HAIN

SAB HASHMI QASAM MERI ISMATH KI KHATE HAIN
MUJH KO ARAB KI RASM SE ANDESHE AATE HAIN
AURATH KA MURDA TAQTH E PA RAKH KAR UTHAATE HAIN
AASAAR IS ME MURDE KE SAB PAAE JAATE HAIN
DETI HOON MAIN QASAM TUMHAIN ROOH -E RASOOL KI
WAALI NA YUN UTTHA -E-YO MAYYATH BATOOL KI

HAI AARZU KE QABR ME MUJH KO HASSAN LITAAE
SHABBIR MERE LAASHEY( MURDE ) KA MU QIBL A ROO PHIRAAE
PHIR QUD KAHA, NAHI NAH I BACHCHA HAI DAR NA JAA YE
NAGAAH KHELTE HUWE DONO YATEEM AAYE
CHAATI LAGA A KE BOLI KE, LO HAM  TOH MARTE HAIN
TUM SE SULOOK DEKHIYE KYA LOG KARTE HAIN

LEYKAR BALAA YEN BETI KI ZEHRA NE YE KAHA
ROTI TO H HOON ZIYADAH NA ZAINAB MUJHE RULAA
KUCH BHAAEYOUN KE SOUNP -NAY KA SAMJHI MUD H-DUAA
TOO UNKI RONE WALI HAI ZEHRA TERE FIDAA
KYA BAS MERA, JO MARZI -E- PARWARDIGA AR HAI
ZAINAB TAMA AM KUNBE KI TOO SOGWAAR HAI

YE KAHTI THI KE GHASH NE DIYA M AUTH KA PAYAAM
HUJRE SE NIKLE ROTE HUWE Y AQROBA TAMAAM
TARTEEB -E- LAASH  KAY LIYE BAHAR GAYE IMAM
ZEHRA NE YAA MUSALLE PA APNA  KIYA MUQAAM
LIKHA HAI BAS NAMAAZ ISHAA KI ADA A HUWI
AUR GHUL UTHA KE BINTE NABI KI QAZAA HUWI
