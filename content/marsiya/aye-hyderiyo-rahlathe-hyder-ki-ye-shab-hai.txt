AYE MATAMIYO ROWO KE MAHSHAR KI YE SHAB HAI
LO WAQEYA -E- FAATHA -E- QAIBAR KI YE SHAB HAI
KUL MOMINOUN KE SAR SE YADULLAH UTTHEGA
TAABOO T-E- JANABE ASADULLAH UTTHEGA

LO MOMINO TUM HOGAYE BE WARIS -O- WAALI
LO DOSTO U HAI TUM SE WIDAA -E- SHAH -E- AALI
KAL SUBHA WO CHILAENGE HYDER KE MAWALI
AAJ AHMED -E- MUQTA AR KI MASNAD HUWI QAALI
TUKDE KIYE ZEHRA NE G IREBAAN -E KAFAN KE
KOI NA RAHA SAR PA HUS SAIN AUR HASSAN KE

MASJID ME SITAMGAAR NE KAABE KO GIRAYA
BE QADR SHAB -E- QADR KA KUCH DHYAAN NA LAYA
MEHRAAB ME QANDEEL -E IMAMATH KO BUJHAYA
SAIDAANIYOUN KO EID KE NAZDIK RULAYA
DARDA SHAHE LOULAAK KE DA -MAAD KO MAARA
FARYAAD KE JIBREEL KE USTAD KO MAAR A

AB WAAQEYA -E- SHERE QUDA KARTA HOON TAHREER
QAATIL KO PAKAD LAY E JU -HI SHABBAR -O- SHABBIR
MASHKEY  THI BA ’NDHI KANPTA THA QOUF SE BEY PEER
LAANATH KI TARHA TEGH THI QAATIL KEY GULOGEER
RAHEM AAGAYA ZALIM KE LARAZ N PE ALI KO
FARMAYA MERE AAGE SE SARKAO SHAQI KO

JAB LE CHALE QATIL KO TO BOLE SHAH -E- ABRAAR
YE DUSHMANI -E SHERE QUDA, AYE SAG -E MAKKAR
IS MAAHE MUBARAK ME KIYA ZULM JAFAAKAAR
KYA TERI IMA MATH KA ALI THA NA SAZAWAAR
KI SHARM NA MAHBOOB -E- QUDA SE NA QUDA SE
LE DEKH YE ROTE HAIN PAYAMBAR KE NA WASE

THA SAATH LAEEN MASJIDE KOOFA SE SARASAR
GHAR LE CHALE SIBTAIN -E- NABI SHAH KO UTHA KAR
THE GIRD TO U IS LAASH KE SAB SHIYYA -E HYDER
FARZAND -E- YATEEM AAGE THE SAR NANGE BARABAR
ZAINAB PE YATIMI KI MUSIBATH JO PADI THI
CHILLATI THI SAR PEET TI THI DA R PA KHADI THI

ABBAS SE HYDER NE KAHA CHUPKE SE JAAO
HAAN FATIMA KI BETI KO DEWDHI SE HATAAO
SAMJHAO KE BAS ARSHE QUDA KO NA HILAAO
RAHGIROUN KO AWAAZ NA RONE KI SUNAAO
EK WAQT YE THA AIK WO AAFATH KI GHADI THI
RAN ME YAHI ZAINAB THI KE SAR NANGE KHADI T HI

YE KAHTE HI BE -CHAIN HUWE HYDER -E- KARRAR
SHABBIR KI GHURBAT PA JIGAR HOGAYA AFKAAR
EK SIMT SE ITNE ME SUNA NOUHA KAYEE BAAR
LETEY THE PA UTT BAITEY , YE KARTE HUWE GUFTAAR
KIS DARD RASIDAH KI YE FARYAAD -O- BUKAA HAI
YE TO MERE ABBAS KE RONE KI SADA HA I

ROKAR KAHA MOULA NE AB AS ISKO HAI WI SWAAS
KYON KYON MERA ABBAS NAHI AATA MERE PAAS
YE SUNKE PHIRE SAR KO JHUK AAE H UWE ABBAS
AUR BAANDH KE HATHOUN KO YE KI ARZ BASAD YAAS
WO DARD HAI MUJHKO KE EFAQA NAHI BABA
IN SOB KA HAI AAQA MERA AAQA NAHI BABA

FAR MAYA ALI NE KE NARO AYE MERE DILDAAR
KYUN ROTE HO JEETHA HAI ABHI HYDER -E- KARRAR
BE WAARIS -O WAALI  TUMHE CHOWDUNGA NA ZINHAAR
LO DOULAT -E- KONAIN TUMHEN DETA HAI GHAFFAR
ALLAH SALAMATH RAKKHE MOULA KO TUMHARE
ROOTHO U NA BULAATA HOON MAIN AAQA KO TUMHARE

PHIR ROKE KAHA RAAZI -E TAQDEER KO LAAO
MAZLOOM KO LAAO SHAH -E- DILGEER KO LAAO
SHAMME LAHA -DE SA AHIBEY TATHEER KO LAAO
SHABBIR KO LAAO, MERE SHABBIR KO LAAO
LAAO USE AAFATH KA FALAK JISPEY GIREGA
SAR JISKA ISI KOOFE ME NEZE PA PHIREGA

YE KAHTE HI SHABBIR SE GHASH KARGAYE HYDER
AUR JAANIB -E- ALLAH -O PAYAMBAR GAYE HYDER
DIDAAR KE PYASE LABE KOUSAR GAYE HYDER
HYDER KE PISAR RONE LAGE MARGAYE HYDER
GHUL PADH GAYA SHAA -HINSHAH -E- ZEEJAAH SIDHARE
JAN NAT KO JAHAN SE ASADULLAH SIDHARE
