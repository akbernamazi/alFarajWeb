
RAN ME ABRU KAMAAN KI AAMAD HAE
ASGHAR -E- BEZABAAN KI AAMAD HAE
BHUKE KI NATAWAAN KI AAMAD HAE
PYAASE KI NEEMJAAN KI AAMAD HAE
YAHI QAIME ME SHOUR HOTA HAE
WADAA UNKA BHI PURA HOTA HAE

PYAAS NE KARDIYA HAI YE AALAM
GHASH PA GHASH AATE HAIN UNHEN PAIHAM
HILTE HAIN HAATH PAAUN BHI KAM KAM
SEENE ME AB NAHI SAMATHA DAM
GHAM SE MATAM ZADOUN KO SAKTA HAE
NANHA SYED PADA SISAKTHA HAE

RO NAHI SAKTE MU BANAATHE HAIN
AANKHEN ULTI HUWI PHIRAATHE HAIN
QUSHK MU, WAA ZABAAN DIKHATE HAIN
GHUL SE RONE KE SAHME JAATHE HAIN
MU KHULA HAE DHADAKTHI CHATHI HAI
SAANS BHI AB ATAKTHI AATI HAI

SEENA GARMI KE MAARE JALTA HAE
NANHA SA DIL PADA UCHALTA HAE
DAM BA DAM RANG KO BADALTA HAE
BAS NAHI KUCH KISI KA CHALTA HAE
NAAM PAANI KA SAB JO LETE HAIN
MU KO GHABRA KE KHOL DETE HAIN

ROKE KAHTI HAE BANO -E DILGEER
MAIN KIDHAR JAAUN KYA KAROON TADBEER
HAAE BACCHE MERE TERI TAQDEER
NAA THO PAANI MILA TUJHE NAA SHEER
HAAL JAB DEKHTHI HAI BA D, ZAINAB
KAHTHI HAI YA ALI MADAD, ZAINAB

HAAE KYA WAQT HAE PADA HAM PAR
KARNE PAA -E- NA MAATAM -E AKBAR
HUWI DARPAESH RAHLATHE ASGHAR
AB MAIN NIKLOONGI ODH KAR CHAADAR
LASHKAR -E- SHAM PAAS JAAUNGI
HAAL BESHEER KA SUNAAUNGI

KAHUNGI JAAKE MAIN HOON SAIDA ANI
DEKHO MARTA HAI YE MERA JAANI
TISHNAGI KI HAI ISPA TUGHYAANI
WAAH KYA QHOOB KI HAI MEHMANI
KARO SAYRAAB LAAL KO MERE
PAANI DO NOUNEHAAL KO MERE

QAIME ME AAE SARWAR -E AALAM
JHULE SE BEZUBAAN KO LAYE HARAM
SHAY NE DEKHA KE HAI LABOUN PAR DAM
ROKE IRSHA D YE KIYA PAIHAM
MERE JAANI MERE JIGAR AAO
CHALO MAIDAN KI SAER KAR AAO

KAHKE YE ROE SARWAR -E- ZEEJAAH
LIYA HATHOUN PA APNE GHIRATH -E- MAAH
KIYA PINHAAN ABAA ME QAATIRQAAH
AUR KAHA LALLAAH – ILLALLAH
LEKE PYASE KO ASHBAAR CHALE
PUSHT QAM KARKE BE QARAAR CHALE

ROKE KAHNE LAGE IMAM -E- GHAYUR
GHAFILO KUCH NAHI HAI TUM KO SHAOOR
TUM SAMAJHNA NAHI QHUDA KO DOOR
MERE BACCHE KA KUCH NAHI HAI QUSOOR
BE GUNAAJ KI NA JAAN LO YAARO
MERE KAHNE KO MAANIO YAARO

ZALIMO WAASTHA PAYAMBAR KA
DAM NIKALTHA HAI MERE DILBA R KA
TAR KARO HALQ MERE ASGHAR KA
JAAM DOONGA MAIN TUM KO KAUSAR KA

TAHRO TAHRO JAWAB MILTA HAI

MAARTHA KYUN HAI BESABAB ZALIM
