
RAN ME JAB SHAH KI TARAF SE HURR -E- DEENDAR AAYA
KIS BASHASHAT SE UDDAA TAA HUWA R EHWAAR AAYA
GHUL HUWA SYED -E-MAZLOOM KA GHAMQHWAAR AAYA
JAAN NISAARE QHALAFE HYDER -E KARRAR AAYA
TATHQHE Y NOOR SARE Y RAAH NAZAR AATA HAE
JALWA -E QUDRAT -E ALLAH NAZAR AATA HAE

YE SUQ AN SUN KE PUKARA PISAR -E SAAD -E S HAREER
HAAN TARAFDAAR -E SH AH-E DEEN PA Y CHALE Y NAI ZA-O- TEER
LEKE HARBOUN KO BADHA FOUJ KA AM BOH -E KASEER
FATEHA PADH KE JAWAN MARD NE KHENCHI SHAMSHEER
HURR KA MU SURQ HUWA FOUJ -E- SITAM ZARD HUWI
SHOLA -E- TEGH SE BIJLI KI CHAMAK GIRD HUWI

BADH KE FARMATE Y THE ABBAS ZAHE Y IZZAT -O- JAAH
BAARE K ALLAH KI DETA THA SADA DILBAR -E  SHAH
KAHTE THE IBN -E- HASAN WAAH HURR -E-GHAAZI WAAH
SHAH HAR ZARB PA FARMATE THE MASHA ALLAH
APNI JAAN -BAAZI KA GHAAZI JO SILAA PAATA THA
MUSKURATA HUWA TASLEEM KO JHUK JAATA THA

HAIF JHUKNE ME LA YEE NOUN NE  JO FURSAT PAAI
SAAMNA CHOD DH KE SAB FOUJ AQAB SE AAI
LAAKH K HOON RE IZ IDHAR AUR UDHAR TANHAAI
BAAG GHODE Y KI PHIRA ATA THA K AY BARCHI KHAA YI
AAGAYA MA UT KE PANJE ME NA KUCH DE YR LAGI
FARQH PAR GURZ LAGA DOSH PA SHAMSHEER LAGI

SEENA GHURBAAL HUWA  TEER CHA LE AADAA KE
RAKHDIYA SHER NE QARBOOZ PA SAR NEODA A KE
ALI AKBAR NE YE HAZRAT SE KAHA CHILLA KE
GAR HO IRSHAAD TO MEHMAAN KO BACHAAUN JAAKE
QAADIM -E- HAZRATE ZEHRA -O- ALI GIRTA HAE
KHAAK PAR AB WO SAEED -E- AZA LI GIRTA HAE

SHAH RONE LAGE YE SUNTE HI MEHMAA N KI QABAR
HOGAI AANSUOUN SE RESH -E- MUBARAK SAB TAR
ALI AKBAR SE KAHA TUM ABHI TAHRO DILBAR
HURR KI IMDAAD KO HAM JAAENGE AYE NOOR -E NAZAR
KIS SE IS WAQT KAHOON MAIN JO Q HALAQ MUJH PAR HAI
LAASH UTHAAUNGA KE MEHMAAN KA HAQ MUJH PAR HAI

YE SUQAN KAH KE C HALE RAN KO JANAB -E- SHABBIR
WAAN H GIRA QHAAK PA GHODE SE HURR -E- BA TOUQEER
DEKH KAR SHAAH KO AATE HUWE BHAAGE BEPEER
PAHONCHE LAASHE PA IMAM -E- DO JAHAN WAQTE AQEER
CHAMAN -E- HASTI -E- MEHMAAN KO UJADTE DEKHA
QHAAK PA AEDIYAAN ZAQMI KO RAGADTE DEKHA

GIR KE LAASHE KE BARA ABAR YE PUKAARE SARWAR
MERA MEHMAAN -O- MADADGAAR -O- MOIN -O- YAAWAR
GURZ KYA TUJHKO LAGA TOOT GAYI MERI KAMAR
GIRPADA GHODE SE AUR AAH NA KI HAMKO QABAR
DOST KE H UJR ME KAB DDOST KO CHAIN AAYA HAI
KHOLDE CHASHM KO BHAI, KE HUSSAIN AAYA HAI

NEEM  WAAH CHASHM SE HURR NE RUQ -E- MOULA DEKHA
ZEEREY  SAR ZANU -E- SHABBIR KA TAKYA DEKHA
MUSKURA KAR TARAF -E- AALAM -E-BAALA DEKHA
SHAH NE FARMAYA KE AYE HURR -E- JARI KYA DEKHA
ARZ KI HUSN -E RUQ H-E H OOR NAZAR AATA HA I
FARSH S E ARSH TALAK NOOR NAZAR AATA H AI

MUJH KO LENE CHALE AATEY  HAIN FARISHTE YA SHAH
MALAKUL MA UT BHI KARTA HAI MOHABBAT KI NIGAAH
QHULD SE SHER -E- KHUDA NIKLE HAIN ALLAH ALLAH
LO BARAMAD H HUWE SHABBAR BHI PIDAR KE HAMRAAH
NANGE SAR AHMED -E- MUQTAR KI PYARI AA YI
DEKHIYE AAP KE NANA KI SAW AARI AAI

KAHKE YE GOD H ME SHABBIR KE LI ANGDAAI
AAYA MAATHE PA ARAQ CHAHRE PA ZARDI CHAAI
SHE H NE FARMAYA HAMEN CHO DD CHALE KYUN BHAI
CHAL BASE Y HURR -E- JAR EE PHIR NA KUCH AAWAZ AAI
TAAI R-E ROOH NE PARWAAZ KI TOOBA A KI TARAF
PUTLIYAAN RAHGA YEEN PHIR  KAR SHAH -E- WALA KI TARAF

ROKEY AKBAR NE KAHA HURR -E- DILAWAR HAI HAI
AYE MADADGA ARE JIGAR BANDE Y PAYAMBAR HA I HAI
KHOON ME SAB TAR HAI TERA ROO HEY MUNAWWAR HAI HAI
