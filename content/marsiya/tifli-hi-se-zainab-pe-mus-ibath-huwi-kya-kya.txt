
ZAINAB PA HUVA KHAATEMA HAR RANJ -O BALAA KA
MAATAM KIYA PEHLAY TOH RASOOL -E DO H SARAA KA
PHIR GHAM HUVA DARPESH BATOOL -E UZARA KA
DEKHA SAR -E AALOODH BA -KHOON SHER -E KHUDA KA
WOH KHASTHAA JIGAR SHABBAR -E DILGEER KO ROYE
PHIR BAAD -E HASAN HAZRATH -E SHABBIR KO ROYE

TIFLI HI SE ZAINAB PE MUSIBATH HUWI KYA KYA
NANA KE LIYE  ROI KIYA M AATAM -E- ZEHRA
AUR MOHSIN -E- MASOOM KI BHI LAASH EY KO DEKHA
YE DAAGH NA BHOOLI THI KE ZAQMI HUWA BABA
TUKDE JIGAR -E- SYED -E- MASOOM KO DEKHA
PHIR TAIGH KE NEECHE SHAH -E- MAZLOOM KO DEKHA

PAAMAL USE PAIKAR -E- QASIM NAZAR AAYA
ZAINAB HI NE KUBRA KO BHI RA ND SAALA PINHAAYA
ATHTHAARA BARAS WAALE KA BHI DAAGH UTHAAYA
AUR LAASHA -E- ASGHAR KO BHI CHAATHI SE LAGAYA
DIL TUKDE HUWA QATL -E- IMAAM -E- MADANI SE
SOORAAQ PADE PUSHT ME NAIZE KI ANI SE

AL QISSA KAY  ZAINAB KO SADAA BHAAI  KI THI YAAD
THA DUSHMAN -E- AAB ID JO YAZEED -E- SITAM EEJAAD
GUZRI YE QABAR  USKO KE KYA BAI TTHA HAI DILSHAAD
IJMAA H HAI SHIYOUN KE PA -YE YAARI -E SAJJAD
WAARIS HAI WO TE IGH -E KAMAR SHAIR -E- QUDA KA
AB TUJH SE IWAZ LE YGA WO H KHOON -E- SHOH ADA KA

MOUJOOD HOON HAAZIR HOON MAI QAIDI HOON TU MHAARA
LEKIN MUJHE BATLAAO YE TUM JALD QUDAARA
ZAINAB KO TO AB QAID KAROGE NA DOBAARA
MOUJOOD HAI WO BHI NAHI KUCH ZOUR HAMAARA
GAR KAAM NAHI IS SE TO KAH DO YE QUSHI SE
MILL AAUN MAE HAMSHEER -E- HUSSAIN IBN -E-ALI SE

AADA NE KAHA JAAO PA JALD AAIYO BAAH AR
AABI D GAYE PAHNE HUWE AAH YE ZALWAR
BAAQER THO GIRE HAAE PIDAR KAHKE ZAMEEN PAR
ZAINAB NE LIPAT KAR YE KAHA KYA HUWA DILBAR
NAACHAAR HAIN KUCH ZOUR HAMAARA NAHIN PYAARE
DURRA THO KISI NE TUMHE MAARA NAHI PYAARE

JAB MANZILE Y AAFATH KI WO THAY KEEN SAH ER-E- SHAAM
PAHONCHA WO GIRAFTAAR -E- BALAA MUTTASIL -E- SHAAM
EK BAAGH ME SHAB BAASH HUWA AAB ID-E- NAAKAAM
WO RAATH THI ZAINAB KE LIYE MA UTH KA PAIGHAAM
ALLAH SE KAHNE LAGI HAATH APNE UTHAA KAR
KYA SHAAM KE BALWE KO MAI PHIR DEKHOONGI JAA KAR

YE SUNKAY D IYA HUKM SHAQI NEY SAR -E DARBAAR
ABID KO GIRAFTAAR KAREIN JAAKAY KUCH ASWAAR
ZANJEER BHI AUR TAUQ BHI BHAARI SAA HO TAYYAAR
LO AB QISAAS -E SHOHADAA KA HAI TALABGAAR
KIS BAATH PAH MAGROOR WOH IBNE SHAHEY DEEN HAI
KYA PEHLI ASEERI KA MAZAA YAAD NAHIN HAI

BAAZU MEY RASAN BAANDHI THI AUR PAAUN MEY ZANJEER
ABID YEHI KEHTHEY THEY KAY KYA HAI MERI TAQSEER
WOH KEHTHEY THEY YAAN POOCHO NA AYE NAAEB -E SHABBIR
AB SHAAM MEY HO JAAYEGI JO HONI HAI TAQDEER
ABID NEY KAHA KHAIR CHALO DARR MUJHEY KYA HAI
MAZLOOM KA FAR ZAND TOH RAAZI BA RIZA HAI

ABID KA SUKHAN KOYI BHI KHAATIR MEY NA LAAYA
NAAQEY PA USI SHAKL SEY BEYKAS KO BITTHAAYA
AUR ROKAY ZANN -O MARD NEY EK SHOR MACHAAYA
NAAGAH YEH EK SHAQS NEY ABID KO SUNAAYA
KYA BAITTEY HO NAAQEY PA ZARAA DEKHO UDHAR KO
HAMSHEER -E HUSSAIN AATHI HAI KHOLEY HUVEY SAR KO

YAA  RAB MUJHE US SHAHER KO AB TOO NA DIKHAANA
SAR NANGE PHIR US MULK ME MUJH KO NA PHIRAANA
HAI YAAD MUJHE SHAAM KA WO RANJ UTHAANA
HAMRAAH SAR SHAH KE HUWA THA MERA AANA
AB PAISH -E- NAZAR BHAI KI TASVEER NAHI HA I
AAYI HOON MY TANHA SAR -E- SHABBIR NAHI HAI

YA BAAR -E- QUDA WAASTHA SHAAHE MADANI KA
SADQA PISAR -E- FATIMA KI BE KAFANI KA
AUR WAASTHA AABED KI GHAREEBUL WATANI KA
SADQA SHAH -E- MAZLOOM KI TISHNA DAHANI KA
ZAINAB TARAF -E- SHAAM YAHAAN SE NA RAWAAN HO
KAL SUBHA MULAAQAAT -E- IMAAM -E- DO JAHAAN HO

US RAATH KO ZAINAB RAHIN MASHGHOOL -E- MUNAAJAAT
NAGAAH SA HER-E KOOCH NUMAAYAAN HUWI HAI HAAT
SAJJAD NE ZAINAB SE YE RO  RO KE KAHI BAAT
LO BHAAI SE TUM APNE KARO AAJ MULAAQAAT
KIS DARD SE KAL AAP THEE  MASHGHOOL -E- DUAA ME
MAQBOOL DUAA HOGAYI DARGAAH -E- QUDAA ME

ZAINAB NE KAHA AAYA YAQEEN AB MUJHE BETAA
PAR MOUTH KE AASAAR THO AB TAK NAHI PAIDAA
AB AAGE YE HAI MUGHBIR -E- AQBAAR NE LIKHAA
IS BAAGH ME BADKHAAH HAI EK AAL -E- NABI KAA
THA BAAGH ME PARQAAR -E- GULIS TAAN -E- JAFAA THA
GULSHAN KI RAWISH BAILCHE SE KHOD RAHAA THA

HAAL US NE SUNA AAMAD -E- ZAINAB KA JO SAARAA
ZAINAB KE QAREEB AAYA WO BE RAHAM QAZAARA
US ZULM KE KAHNE KA ZABAAN KO NAHI YAARAA
BAS PUSHT PE ZAINAB KE WAHI BAILCHA MAARA
ZAINAB NE KAHA HOGA YA SAAMAAN QAZAA KA
MUSHKIL MERI AASAAN HUWI SHUKR QUDA KA
