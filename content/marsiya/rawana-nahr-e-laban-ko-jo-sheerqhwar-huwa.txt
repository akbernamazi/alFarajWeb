
RAWANA NAHR -E- LABAN KO JO SHEERQHWAR HUWA
ZABAAN DEKHANE Y PA GARDAN SE TEER PAAR HUWA
TADAP KE HATHOUN PA HAZRATH KE HAMK ANAAR HUWA
QIZAAN HUWA JO WO GUL  TOU GALE KA HAAR HUWA
IDHAR TO SHAAH KO YE SADMA -E- JIGAR PAHONCHA
UDHAR MADINE SE SUGHRA KA NAAMABAR PAHONCHA

LAHU ME GHARQ KHADE THE KAMAR JHUKA -E- HUWE
PISAR KI NANHI SI MAYYATH GALE LAGAA -E- HUWE
LAHU BHARA HUWA DAAMAN USE UDHAAE HUWE
KAFAN KI FIKR ME Q HAIME KO MU PHIRA -E- HUWE
YE HAA L DEKH KE QA ASID H KI AAS TOOT GAYI
HUWA YE RAASHA KE FOURAN MEHAAR CHOOT GAYI

UTAR KE NAAQHE SE AADA AB WO BAJ AA LAAYA
MAGAR HUSSAIN THE BEHOSH KUCH NA FARMA AYE
WO NOUHA PADHTHA HUWA AUR MUTTASIL AAYA
NIGAAH -E- YAAS SE MU D EKH KAR YE CHILLAYA
QHARAAR DO MUJHE MAIN BEQARAAR HOTA HOON
HUSSAIN JAAN KE TUM PAR NISAAR HOTA HOON

KAHO BAQEE YA-E AULAD -E- MURTAZA HO TUM
AMEER-E- LASHKAR -E- ALLAH -O- MUSTAFA HO TUM
GAREEB -E KOOFA -O- MAZLOOM -E- KARBALA HO TUM
BATAA -O- QASIM -E- NASHAAD KE CHACHA HO TUM
JAWAAB DO PIDAR -E- NAMURA AD-E- AKBAR HO
JANAB -E HAZRATHE ABBAS KE BARA ADAR HO

BAHAN HAI  AAPKI ZAINAB SAKINA DUQTAR HAE
HAR AWWAL AAPK AY LASHKAR KA HURR -E- SAFDAR HAE
WATAN ME BHI KOI BETI ALEEL -O SH ASHDAR HAE
HUZOOR KA KOI NANHA SA LAAL ASGHAR HAE
ZOHAIR AUR HABEEB AAP KE MU SAAHEB HAE
JANAB AUN -O MOHAMMED K AY MAAMU SAHEB HAE

HUSSAIN BHARKE DAM -E SARD BOLE AY GHAM KHAR
AMEER KOUN HAE MAIN TOH HOON BEKAS -O- NACHAAR
KAHAN HAIN AKBAR -O- QASIM KAH AAN ALAMBARDA AR
QHUDA KE BANDE THE YE SAB HUWE E QHUDA PA NISAAR
SIPA AHE ZULM NE TANHA SAMAJH KE GHERA HAI
HAZAROUN TEGH HAI AUR AIK HALQ MERA HAI

HATAA YI CHEHRE SE ASGHAR KE QHOON BHARI CHADAR
PASEENA MAUTH KA MAATHE SE  POUNCHA SAR TA SAR
PUKARE LAASHEY( MURDE ) KI AANKHOUN PA HONTOUN KO RAKH KAR
LIKH I HAE FATIMA NE DEED AA BOSI -E ASGHAR
GAWAH RAHIYO KAY  FAAQE SE JHOOMTHA HAI HUSSAIN
AUR UNKE BHAI KI AANKHON KO CHOOMTHA HAI HUSSAIN

BENA ALOON NANHI SI QABR INKI AYE KHU JIST HA SIYAR
TOH LE CHAL OON  PISAR -E- NOUJAWAN KE LAASHE PAR
TO PADHIYO LASHA -E- AKBAR  PA NAAMA -E- KHAAHAR
YE KAHKE GHODE Y SE UTRE Y IMAM JINN -O- BASHAR
ZAMEEN QABR KI K HATIR PASAND H KARNE Y LAGE
MAZAAR KHOD KE NAALE BALAND KARN EY LAGE
