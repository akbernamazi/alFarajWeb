FARZANDOUN KO PHIR FATIMA ZEHRA NE BULAYA

LO PYARO KE HANGAAM JUDAAI KA AB AAYA

YE HAIN MERE PYARE KABHI INKO NA GHURAKN AA
AUR  AAP SE EK ARZ YE H AB KARTI HAI ZEHRA

FURQAT ME NA IN DONO KO TADPAIYO SAAHEB
BACHHOWN KO MER I QABR PEY  TUM LAAIYO SAAHEB

NAZUK HAI MIZA AJ INKA YE MAALUM HAI MOULA
HAR BAAT ME BE SAAQTA RODE YTA HA I CHOTA
HAI KHOUF ULAT T JAAE NA SEENE ME KALEJA
TUM USKO MERE LAASHEY(MURDE)  PE BHI RONE NA DENA
SAB GOUD H ME LEY  MARTABA QURAAN KA SAMAJH KAR
GHUR -KE NA KOI AB ISE BIN MAA KA SAMAJH KAR

YE KAHKE KIYA BAND DARE HUJRA -E ATHER
SAB KHURD -O-KALAA ’N AAN KE RO NE LAGE BAHER
AAWAZ SUNI KALMAE TAYYAB KI BARA ABAR
PHIR KUCH NA SADA AAI KAHA SAB NE YE ROKAR
RAHLAT HUWI BAS AAJ RASUL -E ARABI KI
LO UTH GAYI DUNYA SE NISHANI BHI NABI KI

PHIR KHOLKE HUJRE KO BASAD NAALAO AFGHAN
MATAM KA KIYA FATIMA KE MURDE PA SAAMAN
FARZANDOUN NE BHI AAKE KIYA CHAAK GAREBAAN
CHILLATE THE QURBAAN HON HAM BOLO TO AMMA
FIZZA NE KAHA FATIMA TO MARGAYI PYARO
AMMA KAHAN AMMA TO QAZAA KARGAI PYARO

AL Q ISSA WO DIN MATAM -E ZEHRA ME JO GUZ RA

MASOOMA KE EK HAATH ME JO ZAQM LAGA THA
WO DASTE BATOOL AAH QAMEE DA NAZAR AAYA
YE DEKHTE HI MU H KO ALI KE JIGAR AAYA
