
QAID QAANE ME TALAATUM HAI KE HIND AATHI HAI
DUQTAR -E- FAATEMA GHIRAT SE MU EE JAATHI HAI
ROOH QAALIB ME W OH ZINDAAN ME GHABRAATHI HAI
BE HAWAASI SE HAR EK BAAR WO H CHILLAATHI HAI
AASMAAN DOOR ZAMIN SAQT KHIDHAR JAAUN MAIN
BIBIYO MILKE DUAA MAANGO KE MAR JAAUN MAIN

KYA KAROON KYA NA KAROON JALD BATAAO LOGO
SADQA AKBAR KA HIQAARATH SE BACHAAO LOGO
OAT T KARKE  HO KHADE MUJH KO CHUPAAO LOGO
YA KISI GOSHE Y ME LEJAAKE BITHAAO LOGO
SAR KHULI HO KISI HUJRE ME MUJHE BAND H KARO
HIND AATHI HAI MUJHE KHAAK KA PAIWAND H KARO

IS TARHA HIND KE AANE SE NA GHABRAAUN MAIN
BINT -E- HYDER HOON NA KYUN QAID ME SHARMAAUN MAIN
KOI DEEWAAR JO SHAQ HOTO SAMAA JAAUN MAIN
SEDHE MAAJAAE KE MAQTAL KO CHALI JAAUN MAIN
KARBALA  ME NA YEH  ZILLAT HAI NA RUSWAAI HAI
BE RIDAA MAIN HOON TO BE GOUR MERA BHAI HAI

NAAGAHAA FIZZA NE DI AHLE - HARAM KO YE QABAR
HIND AATHI HAI BADE JAAH O- TAHAMMUL SE IDHAR
BEERAQAIN NAQRA -O- ZAR KI HAIN JU LOO  KE ANDAR
SAB KANEEZEN TO RIDAA ODHI HAIN WO NANGE SAR
PAR SAWAARI BAHU T AAHISTHA RAWAAN HOTHI HAI
HAR QADAM HIND TE HARR JAATHI HAI AUR ROTI HAI

KAHTHI HAI QAIDIYOUN KE SHOUR -E BUKAA NE MAARA
MUJH KO IS HAAE HUSSAINA KI SADAA NE MAARA
INKE SARDAAR KO KIS AHL -E- JAFAA NE MAARA
KYA WO SYED THA JISE AHL -E- DAGHA NE MAARA
AIK BIJLI SI KALEJE PE MERE GIRTHI HAI
NANGE SAR FAATEMA AANKHOUN KE TALE PHIRTHI HAI

HOGAYA ZIBHA KOI QHAASA -E- BAARI LOGO
INS -O- JIN HOOR -O- MALAK KARTHE HAIN ZAARI LOGO
LECHALO SOO -E- NAJAF MERI SAWAARI LOGO
HOGI MUSHKIL WAHIN AASAN HAMAARI LOGO
QAIR SE HAIN MERE AAQA TO WO SOTE HONGE
WARNA MARQAD H ME ALI BET EY KO ROTHE HONGE

BAR -E- ZINDAAN PA HUWA ITNE ME AMBOUH KAMAAL
BOULE DARBAAN BADH E DOULAT -O- UMR -O- IQBAAL
QAIDIYO UTTHO ADAB SE KARO SAB ISTEQBAAL
ZAN -E- HAAKIM KA HAI ZINDAAN ME NUZOOL -O- AJLAAL
QASR -E- SHAAHI SE JO TASHREET YAHAAN LAATHI HAIN
PARWARISH KARNE KO TUM SAB KI HUZOOR AATHI HAIN

LOUNDIYAAN THIN ZAN -E- HAAKIM KAY  JULOO  ME JO RAWAAN
DEKHTHI KYA HAIN KE EK SHER HAI AAHAN ME NIHAAN
LAAGHAR -O- QASTA TAN -O- FAAQAKASH -O- TISHNA DAHAAN
MOO H PA SA ILI KE NISHAAN PUSHT PA DURROUN KE NISHAAN
SAAQ AY PA FAAQE SE ZANJEER  MEY  THARRAT HI HAI
ISTHEQANOUN SE LARAZNE KI SADAA AATHI HAI

SAB NE MU KARKE SOO -E- HIND YE KI NOUHA GIRI
DEKHIYE SHAAM KE ZINDAAN ME CHIRAAGHE SAHARI
YAADE QAALEQ ME JO HAI QALQ SE HAI BE QABARI
KYUN MUSALSIL KIYA YE TO HAI ADAM KA SAFARI
KIS NE DAM BAND KIYA TOUQ -E- JAFAA SE ISKA
SILSILA MILTHA HAI KUCH SHAIR -E KHUDA SE ISKA

GIRD AABI D KE PHIRI HIND BA HAAL -E- TAAGHEER
RAKH DIYA PAAUN PA SAR APNA HATAAKAR ZANJEER
BOLE WO H KAUN HAI BOLI KE KANEEZ -E- SHABBIR
AASALAAM AYE RASAN -O- TOUQ -O- SALAASAL KE ASEER
HAI WASIYYATH KA MAHAL MARNE PA TAYYAR HAI TOO
KUCH KAFAN KE LIYE RAKHTHA HAI KE NAADAAR HAI TOO

GHAM NA KAR GOUR -O- KAFAN MAI TUJHE DOONGI LILLAH
NANGE SAR TERE JANAAZE KE CHALOONGI HAMRAAH
MARNE WAALE TERAA KYA NAAM HAI KAB SE HAI TABAA H
BOLE MOULA ABHI CHAALEES BARAS JEENA HAI AAH
NAAM BEKAS BHI HAI Q AIDI BH I HAI BEEM AAR BHI HAI
HAAL YE H HAI KE ASEER BHI HAI AAZAAR BHI HAI

HIND NE POOCHA  MARAZ KYA HAI KAHA BE PIDARI
ROKE WO BOLI DAWAA KYA HAI KAHA NOUHAGIRI
GHAR KO DARYAAFT KIYA KAHNE LAGE DARBA DARI
BOLI LETA HAI QABAR KOUN KAHA BE QABARI
AAH KARNEY  KA SABA B POOCHA TO SHARMAANE LAGE
TAAZIYAANOUN KE NASHAAN PUSHT PA DIKHLAANE LAGE
