
JAB MAHFIL -E YAZEED ME DAAQIL HARAM  HUWE
GHASH AAYA HAR QADAM PA YE ZULM -O SITAM HUWE
ZAINAB PUKAARI HAADESE AISE BHI KAM HUWE
SAR KAT GAYA HUSSAIN KA SAR NANGE HAM HUWE
KYUN AASMAAN GIR NAHI PADHTHA DUHAAI HAI
ZEHRA KI BETI SAAMNE HAAKIM KE AA YI HAI

MOUJOOD ANJUMAN ME SAGHEER -O KABEER HAIN
HAAKIM KE GIRD KURSIYOUN PAR SAB AMEER HAIN
QAASAAN -E ZULJALAAL ZALEEL -O HAQEER HAIN
MUSHKIL KUSHA KE QURD -O KALAAN SAB ASEER HAIN
KEHDE KOI ALI SE YE DAARUS - SALAAM ME
ZAINAB KA DAAQELA HUWA DARBAAR -E AAM  ME

AYE LOGO KARBALA SE MERE BHAAI KO BULAAO
AYE LOGO MERE GESUOUN WAALE JAWAAN KO LAAO
AYE LOGO MERA HAADESA ABBAS KO SUNAAO
AYE LOGO YAADGAAR -E HASSAN SE KAHO KE AAO
QAASIM SE HAAE KUCH NA KAMAK US GHADI HUWI
YE BAAL KHOLE KIS KI DULHAN HAI KHADI HUWI

HOKAR ASEER DUQTAR -E KHAAT AM JO AA YI THI
NAANA NE MERE QUD H I SEY CHAADAR UDHAAI THI
POSHAAK AMMA NE NAYI  ISKO PINHAAI THI
SAB KO YE PAAS THA KE Y SAQI KI WO JAA YI THI
UMMATH KO HAAE PAAS RASOOL -E QUDA NAHI
ZAINAB BARAHNA SAR HAI AUR UNKO HAYA NAHI

BABA KO MERAY POOCH TOH KAISA SAQI HAI WOH
MUSHKIL KUSHA HAI KUL KA KHUDA KA WALI HAI WOH
NAANA KO MEREY DEKHO KAY SAB KA WALI HAI WOH
BHAAI KAY SADQEY FAQR -E NABI -O ALI WOH
MERI KAHBAR ASEERI MEY LEYTHA NAHIN KOYI
KAPDAA BHI MUH CHUPAANEY KO DEYTHA NAHIN KOY I

DARBAAN MEY YAZEED KAY HARSOO THA IZDIHAAM
TEHRAA RAHEY THEY BAITH KAY MAANIND KHAAS -O AAM
BAITTA THA TAQTH -E NEHAS PAH WOH NUTHFAA -E HARAAM
EK HAATH MEY TOH SHEESHA THA EK HAATH MEY THA JAAM
BAALAA -E TAQTH SHUGHAL THA ISKO SHARAAB KA
AUR ZEIR -E TAQTH  SAR THA KHALAF -E BUTURAAB KA

HAAZIR WAHAAN THA EK FARANGI BHI US GHADI
ROKAR KAHAA YAZEED SEY ISNEY KAY AYE SHAQI
TERAY MAAMLEY SEY LARAZTHA HAI MERA JI
SAR HAI TABAQ MEY JISKA YEH MUJRIM HAI KYA KOYI
HAASHA JO MEY KAHOON KAY YEH AHLEY QASOOR HAI
ZULFO UN MEY BOO -EY MASHK HAI CHEHRAY PAH NOOR HAI

TEREY MAAMLEY SEY MERA HOUSH JAATA HAI
KYA HARF -E BADH TUJHEY YEH KATTA SAR SUNAATHA HAI
JO CHOOB BAIDH ISKAY LABBOUN PAR LAGAATHA HAI
KOYI BHI HAATH MURDEY KAY SAR PAR UTTHAATHA HAI
ISKA GUNAAH TOH MUJHEY HAI RAAN KAR GAYAA
BAAQI QASAAS REHGAYA AUR SAR UTAR GAYA
