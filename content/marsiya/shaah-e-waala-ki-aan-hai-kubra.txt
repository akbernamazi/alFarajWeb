SHAAH -E- WAALA KI AAN HAI KUBRA
AAL -E- ZEHRA KI SHAAN HAI KUBRA
ZEENAT -E- KHAANDAN HAI KUBRA
BANO -E- SHAH KI JAAN HAI KUBRA
PAR -E-QAIBE SHAH -E- DEEN HAI YE
SANIYE ZAINAB -E- HAZEEN HAI YE

JAD PAYAMBAR HAI AHMED -E- MUQTAR
DADA HYDER HAI MAZHAR -E- GHAFFAR
DADI ZEHRA RASOOL KI DILDAAR
BABA SHABBIR QULD KE SARDAAR
INKE GHAR ME KITAAB AAI HAI
ZULM UMMATH INHI PA DHAAI HAI

KYA KYA BIBI NE DEKHE RANJ -O- GHAM
TUKDE QALB -O- JIGAR HUWE PAIHAM
BAZU ABBAS KE HAI DEKHE QALAM
BAAP KA DEKHI SAR SINAAN PA ALAM
ZAQMI AKBAR KA HAI JIGAR DEKHI
LAASH -E- ASGHAR KO KHOON ME TAR DEKHI

HAAE KUBRA KI KYA RACHI SHAADI
SHAADI KAISI THI WO THI BARDAADI
BAHOT DULHA KI LAASH DIKHLADI
PEET THI SAR RAHI WO SHAHZADI
RAN ME PAMAAL HOGAYA NOUSHAH
HOGAYI LAMHOUN KI DULHAN BEWAH

AIK DIN ME HAI KYA MAHEN DEKHI
LUTTHA ZEHRA KA HAI CHAMAN DEKHI
DASTHE ZAINAB ME HAI RASAN DEKHI
BAAP KI LAASH BEKAFAN DEKHI
KYA KYA JORO JAFAA SAHI KUBRA
BAAP KO BHI NA RO SAKI KUBRA

ZULM SHAM -E- GHARIBAAN AADA KE
SAAM NE AANKHOUN KE HALE QAIME
SAR SE CHAADAR LAEEN CHEEN LIYE
PUSHTE AABID PA HAI LAGE DURRE
MAA PHUPI KO HAI BERIDDAA DEKHI
TOUQ ME BHAAI KA GALAA DEKHI

KYA KYA ZULM -O- SITAM SAHI KUBRA
NANGE SAR BALWE ME PHIRI KUBRA
BHARE DARBAAR ME GAYI KUBRA
QOON KE ASH K ROTHI THI KUBRA
GHAM SAHI SAATH SAATH ZAINAB KE
QAID THI SAATH SAATH ZAINAB KE

GHAMZADA SHAAH -E- DEEN KI DUQTAR
ZULM UMMATH KE SAHKE NOUHAGAR
AAJ MOMIN WO BANO KI DILBAR
KOOCH DUNYA SE KARGAYI AAQIR
GHAM RASIDA GUZAR GAYI KUBRA
PEETO SAR SHIYYO MARG AYI KUBRA
