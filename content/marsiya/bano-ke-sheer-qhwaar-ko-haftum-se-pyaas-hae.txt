
BANO KE SHEER QHWAAR KO HAFTUM SE PYAAS HAE
BAC HCHE KI NABZ DEKH KE MAA BE -HAWAAS HAE
NAA DOODH HAI NAA PAANI KE MILNE KI AAS HAE
PHIRTHI HAI AAS PAAS YE JEENE SE YAAS HAE
KAHTHI THI KYA KAROON MAIN DUHAAI HUSSAIN KI
PUTHLI PHIRI HAI AAJ MERE NOOR -E- AIN KI

FARYAAD YA ALI MAIN KIDHAR JAAUN YA ALI
IN DAAGHOUN KO KAHAN SE JI GAR LAAUN YA ALI
KIS TARHA INKI SAANS KO T EHRAAUN YA ALI
PAANI KA QHAHAT HAI MAIN KAHAN PAAUN YA ALI
PICHLI KO AANKH KHOLE THE AB KHOLTHE NAHI
ROTE NAHI HUMAKTHE NAHI BOLTHE NAHI

AAQIR KAHA YE SAB NE, BULAAO IMAM KO
LAAO KHUDA KE WAASTHE LAAO IMAM KO
IS B EZABAAN KA HAAL SUNAAO IMAM KO
NEELI RAGEN GALE KI DIKHA AO IMAM KO
AKBAR KI LAASH LE GAYE HAIN QATLGAAH ME
KOI PUKAR  LO WO H ABHI HONGE Y RAAH ME

MU PAR JAWAAN BETE KA TAZA LAHU LAGAAE
MATAM SARAA ME GANJE SHAHIDAAN SE SHAAH AA YE
JHULE PA HAATH PAKDE HUWE A HLEBAITH LA YE
BAC HCHE KE HAATH PAAUN HILA KAR INHE Y DIKHAA YE
ROKAR KAHA KE SANS FAQATH AASHKAAR HAI
HO ISKA KYA HISAAB KE DAM KA SHUMAAR HA I

BAITHE SARHAANE JHULE KE SHABBIR SAR JHUKAA YE
ASGHAR KE KAAN SE LAB -E MOUJIZ NUMA MILAAE
CHUPKE SE KUCH KAHA KE WO  SUNTE HI MUSKURAA YE
SOO -E- HUSSAIN HAATH BHI BE SAAQTHA BADHAAE
BOLI SAKINA BABA NE MUSHKIL KUSHAA YI KI
AMMA MUBARAK AANKH KHULI MERE BHAAI KI

HATHOUN PA I SKO LEKE CHALE SHAAH -E KARBALA
AUR SAATH SAATH GOUD H KO KHOLE HUWE QHAZAA
LIKHA HAE DHOOP TEZ Z THI AUR GARM THI HAWA
ASGHAR PA MAA  NE DAAL DI UJLI SI EK RIDAA
CHAADAR NA THI WO CHAHRA -E PUR AAB -O- TAAB PAR
TUKDA SUFAID ABR KA THA AAFTAAB PAR

PAHONCHE QAREEB -E- FOUJ TO TYOR AA KE REH GAYE
CHAAHA KARE SAWAAL PA SHARMA KE REH GAYE
GHAIRATH SE RANG U DD GAYA  THARRA KE RAH GAYE
CHAADAR PISAR KE CHEHRE SE SARKA KE RAH GAYE
AANKHEN JHUKA KE BOLE YE HAM INKO LAA YE HAIN
ASGHAR TUMHAARE PAAS GHARZ LEKE AA YE HAIN

PHIR HONT BEZUBAAN KE CHOOME JHUKA KE SAR
ROKAR KAHA JO KAHNA THA SO KAH CHUKA PIDAR
BAAQI RAHI NA BAAT H KOI AYE MERE PISAR
SOOKHI ZABAAN TUM BHI DIKHADO NIKAAL KAR
PHE RI ZABAN LABOUN PA JO IS NOOR -E- AIN NE
THARRA KE AASMAAN KO DEKHA HUSSAIN NE

MOULA FALAK KO DEKH RAHE THE KE NAAGAHAAN
LI HURMULA NE SHAANE SE DO TAAN K KI KAMAAN
TARKASH SE JAB KE KHEENCH L IYA TEER -E JAAN FISHAAN
CHO DDA KAMAAN SE TAAK -KE HALQOOME BE ZUBAAN
CHUT -THE HI HALQ BACH CHE KA CHE YDAA JO TEER NE
THARRA KE AASMAN KO DEKHA SAGHEER NE

KYA SIN THA TEER KHATE Y HI BAC HCHAA DAH EL GAYA
SOOKHE GALE SE KHOON KA DARYA UBAL GAYA
TADPA JO SHAY KE  HATHOUN PA TO MANKA DHAL GAYA
TOPI GIRI ZAMEEN PA AUR DAM NIKAL GAYA
NANHI KALA IYOUN ME TASH -NUJ H SE BAL PADE Y
HICHKI JO AAI MU SE ANGOTTE NIKAL PADE Y

MU A ASMAAN SE SH EH NE PHIRAYA KE KYA HUWA
DEKHA KE PAAR HALQ SE TEER -E JAFAA HUWA
BAC HCHA TADAP RAHA HA I LAHU ME BHARA HUWA
AUR NANHA HAATH ZAQME GULU PAR DHARA HUWA
AANKHEN PHIRAAE DETE HAIN TEYWAR BADALTHE HAIN
AAGE TO DOODH UGLE Y THE Y AB KHOON UGALTHE Y HAIN

KHAINCHA GALE SE BAC HCHE KEY AAHISTHA SHAY NE TEER
AUR HATOUN PE BU LAND H KIYA LAASHA -E SAGHEER
GARDAN JHUKA KE BOLE KE AYE KHALIQ H-E QADEER
MAQBOOL HO HUSSAIN KA YE FIDYA -E- AQEER
SHASHMAHA KOI KUSHTA -E TEERE SITAM NAHI
YE BEZUBAAN NAAQA -O SAALEH SE KAM NAHI
