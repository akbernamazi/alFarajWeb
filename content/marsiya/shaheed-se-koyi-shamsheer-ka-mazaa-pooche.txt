
SHAHEED SE KOYI SHAMSHEER KA MAZAA POOCHE
MAREEZ SE KOI AKSEER KA MAZAA POOCHE
ASEER SE KOI ZANJEER KA MAZAA POOCHE
YATEEM SE KOI DILGEER KA MAZAA POOCHE
HAR EK PA GUZRA ZAMANE ME RANJ -O- GHAM HAE HAE
SAHAA NA AABID -E- BEEMAR SA ALAM HAE HAE

SHAHEED HOTE HUWE BAAP KO PISAR DEKHE
ANI PA NAIZE KI HAE HAE SAR -E- PIDAR DE KHE
JAWAAN BHAAI KA NIKLA HUWA JIGAR DEKHE
PHUPI KO AANKHOUN SE BALWE ME NANGE SAR DEKHE
KISI NABI PE NA ISTARHA KE ALAM GUZRE
JO IS MAREEZ PA HAR TARHA KE SITAM GUZRE

SHADEED TIP ME WO KAANTOUN PA CHALNA AABID KAA
PAHAN KE BEDIYAAN HAR EK QADAM PA GIR PADNAA
WO ZULM -O- JOUR -E- SITAMGAR HARAM KA SHOUR -E- BUKAA
WO JISM -E- ;AAGJARE AABDO WP DHOOP WO DURRA
SIWAAYE SHUKRE QUDA KUCH NA KAHTHE THE SAJJAD
DUAA -E- UMMATH -E- AASI ME RAHTHE THE SAJJAD

PHIRAAYA KAANTOUN PA SAJJAD KO BARAHNAA PAA
NABI KE KUNBE K O BALWE ME LEGAYE AADAA
JALAAYA QAIMA -E- NAAMOOSE SYED -E- WAALA
PHIRAAKE BALWE ME ZINDAAN ME QAID KARDAALA
TAMAASHA DEKHNE SAB AHLE SHAAM AATHE THE
HAYAA SE AHLE HARAM GARDAN -E JHUKAATHE THE

SITAM KE BAAD HUWA QAID SE JO CHUTKAARA
LAEEN SE BAAP KA SAR I S YATEEM NA MAANGA
SARE HUSSAIN QAZAANE SE HIND NE BHEJA
SAWAAR OONTOUN PA KUNBA HUWA PAYAMBAR KA
LAHAD ME LAASH GHARIBOUN KI HAAE DHAME CHALE
MAH -E- BUTOOL KO PALVAND -E- KHAAK KARNE CHALE

JO PAHONCHE KARB -O- BALA AABIDE ASEERE BALAA
PIDAR KO DEKE KAFAN  KHAAK ME RAKHA LAASHA
LIPAT KE QABR SE CHILLAAE YUN KE AYE BABA
WATAN KO JAATHA HOONN LO AAQRI MERA MUJRAA
WATAN SE AAYA THA KIS SHAAN SE YAHAAN HAE HAE
LUTAA KE GHAR KO MAI JAATA HOON NEEM JAAN HAE HAE

LIKHA HAI YUN KE WATAN AAYE JAB SHAH -E-WAALA
PIDA R KI YAAD ME HAR WAQT KARTHE THE NAALA
NAZAR JO AATHA THA PAANI THO ROTE THE MOULA
HAMESHA AANKHOU  ME PHIRTHA THA DASHT -E- KARB -O- BALA
PIDAR KE BAAD BHI RAAHAT MILI NA AABID KO
DO BAARA QAID KIYA IS GHAREEB ZAAHED KO

WATAN SE LEGAYE PHIR QAID KARKE BA ANIYE SHAR
HUWI BHATIJE KE HAMRAAH ZAINAB -E- MUZTAR
QAREEB SHAAM JO PAHONCHAA WO DONO NOUHA GAR
LAGAAYA BELCHA ZAINAB KI PUSHT KE OOPAR
SAFAR ME ZAINAB -E- BEKAS SE CHUT GAYE SAJJAD
DAYAAR -E- SHAAM ME PHIR AAKE LUT GAYE SAJJAD

PHUPI KO SOUNP KE JANGAL ME  SYED -E- WAALA
MADINE ME HUWA DAAQIL WO BEKAS -O- TANHA
WALEED -E- SHAAM KE HAAKIM NE ZAHER BHIJWAAYA
WO SAM KE KHAATE HI AABID NE KI JAHAAN SE QAZAA
FALAK PA HOOR -O- MALAK ARSH KO HILAANE LAGE
ZAMEEN PA JINN -O- BASHAR SAR PA QAAK UDAANE LAGE

MOHIBBO DAAG HE ALAM DIL PA DHAR GAYE SAJJAD
JO KHAYA ZAHER TO SAD HALF MARGAYE SAJJAD
NABI KI AAL KO BE AAS KARGAYE SAJJAD
YATEEM HOGAYE BAAQER GUZAR GAYE SAJJAD
SAR APNA PEET THE MARQAD SE MUSTAFA NIKLE
KALEJA THAAMKE KYUN KAR NA FAATEMA NIKLE

CHARAAGHE DEEN -E- NABI AAJ BUJH GAYA YAA RAB
NISHAAN -E- PANJETAN -E- PAAK MITGAYA YAA RAB
UTHAA JAHAAN SE SARTAAJ AB MERA YAA RAB
QITAAB BEWA -E- AABID MUJHE MILA YAA RAB
KAROON MAI KYA MERI QISMATH BIGADH GAYI YAA RAB
CHAMAN ME FAATEMA KE OUS PADH GAYI YAA RAB

QUDA KE ARSH KA TAARA GUZAR GAYA LOGO
GHAZAB HAI SHAAH HAMAARA GUZAR GAYA LOGO
WO BEKASOUN KA SAHAARA GUZAR GAYA LOGO
HASSAN HUSSAIN KA PYAARE GUZAR GAYA LOGO
MADINA LUT GAYA YA MUSTAFA DUHAAI HAI
NAJAF UJAD GAYA YA MURTAZA DUHAAI HAI
