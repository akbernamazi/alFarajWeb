AZEEZO HAADESA NOU FALAK DIKHATA HAI
HARAM KA QAAFELA PAISHE YAZEED JAATHA HAI
GALE BANDHE HAIN BADAN SAB KA THAR THARAATHA HAI
NA SAANS LETE HAIN QAIDI NA BOLA JAATHA HAI
WO GIRTHE HAIN TO SITAMGAAR NAIZE MAARTHE HAIN
WO ROKE HYDER -E- KARRAR KO OUKAARTHE HAIN

KAHOON ASEEROUN KA SAB HAAL YE MAJAAL HAI KYA
YE HAATH BANDHNE KA MAZMOON HAI DAST BASTHA KHADA
HUWA HAI BEDIYOUN SE BAS KE GHULGHULA PAIDA
BUTOOL QUID ME HYDER KO DERAHI HAI SADA
BULAND KARTHI HAI FARYAAD YA ALI ZAINAB
CHALO YAZEED KE DARBAAR ME CHALI ZAINAB

NAYI JAFAA HAI KE BAARA GALE ME AIK RASAN
AUR US RASAN PA HAI TOUQ -E- GIRAAN KA RANJ -O- MAHAN
KAHIN SUNA HAI KE DO TEEQH AUR EK  GARDAN
AUR AIK PAAUN ME BHAARI DO HALQAA -E- AAHAN
JO PAAS AAKE LAEEN NAIZOUN SE DARAATHE HAIN
QUDA KE WAASTHE BACCHE UNHAIN DILAATHE HAIN

JAFAA -E- RAAH KE KAHNE ME KAANPTHI HAI ZABAAN
GHARZ KE DAAQELA MAJLIS HUWE BA AAH -O- FUGHAAN
MAGAR KAB AAYE HUZOOR  YAZEED -E- BE EIMAAN
KE CHOWB -E- BAID THI AUR SHAAH KE LAB -O- DANDAAN
CHADI TO AIK THI PAR ZULM DO NUMAAYAAN THA
BA ZAIR -E- CHOUB KABHI LAB THE SHAH KE DANDAAN THE

YE ZULM DEKH KE ZAINAB NE KARLIN AANKHEN BAND
PUKAARA SHIMR YE DARBAAR KYA NA AAYA PASAND
LO AANKHEN KHOLO NAHI ZULM HOWEGA DAH CHAND
FALAK KO DEKH KE RONE LAGI WO GHEERATH MAND
KAHAA YAZEED KYA KYA AASMAAN KO DEKHA
WO ROKE BOLI KE QAALEQ KI SHAAN KO DEKHA

KAHA YAZEED NE TAB SHUMR SE BA TAISH TAMAAM
YE KOUN HAI KE JO BADH BADH KE KAR RAHI H AI KALAAM
WO BOLI POOCH MUJH SE YE JAANE KYA MERA NAAM
MAI RONE WAALI HOON KUNBE KI ZAINAB -E- NAAKAAM
ALI KI AAI NIDAA MERI PYAARI DUQTAR HAI
SAR -E- HUSSAIN PUKAARA HAMAARI QWAAHAR HAI

YAZEED JAAEZA PHIR QAIDIYOUN KA LENE LAGAA
NAZAR PADI USE EK SHAASHZ AADI MAAH LAQAA
ULAT KE DAALA HAI MU PAR PHATA HUWA KURTHAA
AUR USPA HAATHOUN SEE PARDA KIYA HAI CHEHRE KA
HAI SIN ME QURD BUZURGOUN KA PAR QAREENA HAI
KAHA YE OU HAI BOLA UMAR SAKINA HAI

YAZEED HAAL -E- SAKINA PA ROYA AUR YE KAHAA
SAKINA TUJH KO BAHOT C HAAHTHA THA BAAP TERA
WOBOLI CHAAHNA KAISA PIDAR THE MUJH PA FIDAA
ZABAAN PAR THA SAKINA SAKINA SUBH -O- MASAA
SULAATHE THE MUJHE SHABBIR APNE DAAMAAN ME
AUR AB TO KHAAK PA SOTI HOON ROZ ZINDAAN ME

YAZEED NE KAHA JANNAT ME MEWE HAIN AAKAR
SAKINA TUM KO K HILAATA THA KYA TUMHARA PIIDAR
RATAB RATAB KAHA IS BE PIDAR NE TOTLA KAR
DEKHA KE NEEL TAMAANCHOUN KE BOLI DEKH IDHAR
PIDAR NE TOHFE SE TOHFEA RATAB KHILAAYE HAIN
AUR UNKE BAAD TO HAMNE TAMAANCHE KHAAE HAIN

YAZEED BOLA RATAB DOON TO KHAAEGI NAADAAN
SAKI NA BHUKI TO THI HOKE QUSH PUKAARI KE HAAN
MAGAR SAKINA KI QAATIR WAHAAN RATAB THE KAHAAN
DIYA SHAQI NE TABAQ ME SAR -E- IMAAME ZAMAAN
PUKAARI HISSE ME MERE RATAB YE AAYA HAI
KISI YATEEM NE LOGO YE MEWAA KHAAYA HAI
