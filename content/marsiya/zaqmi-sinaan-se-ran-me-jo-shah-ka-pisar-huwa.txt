
ZAQMI SINAAN SE RAN ME JO SHAH KA PISAR HUWA
HAR SIMT SE LAEEN HAR EK HAMLAWAR HUWA
TAR QH OON ME MURAQQA -E- QAIRUL BASHAR HUWA
AAQIR JUDA IQAAB SE WO SEEMBAR HUWA
MAQTAL ME KYA HUJOOM THA IS NOOR -E- AIN PAR
PARWANE GIR RAHE THE CHARAGH -E- HUSSAIN PAR

GIRNA THA BAS KE SAR PA LAGA GURZ HAE SITAM
YUN JHUK GAYE KE HOTE HAIN SAJDE ME JAISE QAM
RAKHDI GALE PA SHEESH NE SHAMSHEER -E- TEZ DAM
TALWAR EK PADI KE HUWI PASLIYAAN QHALAM
GHUL THA KARO NA RAHAM TAN -E- PAASH PAASH PAR
DOWDHA DO GHODE AKBAR -E- MAHRU KI LAASH PAR

HAZRATH KHADE THE QAIME KI PAKDE HUWE TANAAB
SUN KAR YE GHUL RAHI NA DIL -E- NAA TAWAAN KO TAAB
NAAGAAH AAI RAN SE SADAA -E- FALAK JANAAB
BETA JAHAN SE JAATA HAE AB AAIYE SHITAAB
LAASHE PA ZULM -0- JOUR -O- BAD AFFAL KARTE HAIN
GHODOWN SE AHL -E- KEEN HAMEN PAMAAL KARTE HAIN

SUNKAR YE ISTAGHASA -E- FARZAND -E- QUSHQISAAL
SYED NE AAH KI KE  HILAA ARSH -E- ZULJALAAL
KHOLE JANAAB -E- FATEMA KI BETIYOUN NE BAAL
BANO PUKARI QAIR TO HAI AYE ALI KE LAAL
HAE HAE PISAR SE KOUNSI MAADAR BICHAD GAYI
SAAHEB BATAAO KYA MERI BASTI UJAD GAYI
