AUR PURZE PURZE TAN ME LIBAAS -E KOHAN HUWA

DAST -E ALAM SE CHAAK NABI KA KAFAN HUWA
ZAQMOUN KE MU SE QHOON KE DARYA UBAL PADE
SAR NANGE QABR SE SHAH -E MARDAAN NIKAL PADE

MAZLOOM KAHTHA JAATHA THA MAIN BE GUNAAH HOON
MAARO NA MUJH KO SIBT -E RISAALATH PANAAH HOON
TUM LOGOUN KA QUDA KI QASA M QAIR QWAAH HOON
IS KHOWL PAR QUDA KO MAIN RAKHTHA GAWAAH HOON
KUCH RAHAM AATHA THA NA KISI NAABAKAAR KO
SHAMSHEEREN MAARTHE THE GHAREEBUD DAYAAR KO

NEZAA LAGA JO SEENE PA THARRA KE RAH GAYE
BAITHA JO TEER MAATHE PA TEOURAA KE RAH GAYE
GIRNE LAGE TO HAAT HOUN KO LATKAAKE RAH GAYE
SHUKR -E QUDA ZUBAAN SE FARMAAKE RAH GAYE
AKBAR NA THE JALUH ME NA ABBAS PAAS THE
MAZLOOM BEECH ME THA ADOO AAS PAAS THE

SEENE PA BHALA RAKH KE GIRAAYA HUSSAIN KO
JI BHAR KE ZAALIMOUN NE SATAAYA HUSSAIN KO
GIRNE PA KHAAK THODA BAN AYA HUSSAIN KO
HAI ZULM KI YE HAD KE GHASH AAYA HUSSAIN KO
PAR DEKHIYO HAWAAS SHAHINSHAAH -E NAIK KE
SAJDE ME SAR JHUKAA -DIYA HAATHOUN KO TAYK KE

AAYA SARHAANE TEGH BAKAF SHIMR -E RO SIYAAH
BOLA GALA KE MAI HOON PAYAMBAR KI BOSAGAAH
DIL NE KAHA KE SEENA HAI  GANJEENA -E ILAAH
BAITHA YE US JAGA KE UNHAIN JAAE SHARAH AAH
IS ZULM -E- NOU SE CHARQ -E KOHAN KAANPNE LAGA
AISA HUSSAIN TADPE KE RAN KAANPNE LAGA

PAANI WO MAANGTHE HAIN PILAATHA NAHI KOI
IS DARD KO QAYAAL ME LAATHA NAHI KOI
KYA QAHAR HAI KE PAAS BHI AATHA  NAHI KOI
SEENE SE BE HAYAA KO UTHAATHA NAHI KOI
AMMA TUMHAARI PEET -THI HAI BILBILAATI HAI
WO BETE SE LIPAT THI HAIN HOOREN CHUDHAATHI HAI
